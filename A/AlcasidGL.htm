career:

Of the zoological staff of the Philippine National Museum, Manila

 

Collecting localities:

He made a study and collection of natural history specimens, e.g. of plants in Palawan (March 30-June 18, 1938); in 1939 he made two joint botanico-zoological expeditions with Capco & Edaño to Baler and vicinity (March, May-June) in NE. Luzon. Accompanied the Chicago-Philippine Expedition headed by Dr Harry Hoogstraal (see there), and collected plants in Abra Prov., Luzon, in Apr.-May 1946, and not in 1947-48.

 

collections:

In Herb. Manila [PNH] and Herb. Chicago [F], > 300 nos, at least partly with, B.S. numbers (cf. sub Bureau of Science, Manila). Abra: 327 nos in the P.N.H. series [PNH].
