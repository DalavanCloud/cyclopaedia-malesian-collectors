Alberto

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Fortunate R. Alberto coll. for Bur. of Sci. Manila, acc. to Burkill.

 

Collecting localities:

Merrill cites a no 36 collected by Alberto, in his ‘Enum. Philip. Fl. Pl.’; no A 328, Dioscorea elmeri var. vera Prain & Burk., was collected at Los Baños (cf. Prain & Burkill, The genus Dioscorea, 1936, p. 181); and other plants from Los Baños, Luzon, about 1905.
