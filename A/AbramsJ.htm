Abrams, J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A sergeant of the Forest Guards, and later Forest Ranger in Penang, 1888-1910.

 

Collecting localities:

Obtained specimens for Ch. Curtis (see there) in the Malay Peninsula (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).
