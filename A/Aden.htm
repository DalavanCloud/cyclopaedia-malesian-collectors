Collecting localities

 

Aden

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1926, Bogor, Java, Indonesia. Died: July 22, 1953, Tjibeureum above Tjibodas, W. Java

 

Collecting localities:

Together with Main, assisted Kostermans on an expedition to Morotai, Moluccas, from April-June 1949.
