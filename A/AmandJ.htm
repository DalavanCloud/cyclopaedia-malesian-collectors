Collections

 

Amand, J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Resident at Blitar (Kediri), E. Java, sent plants to Hort. Bog. in 1875. 

 

collections:

In the lists of the material preserved in Herb. Berl. [B] a collection of mosses from Banka is mentioned (presented by J. Amand a. 1858), this is apparently based on a mistake and should be J. Amann (= S. Kurz, see there); the same holds true for collections in Herb. Leiden [L], and Utrecht [U].
