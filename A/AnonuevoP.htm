Añonuevo, Prudencia

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1926, Bucalbucalan, Sorsogon, Luzon, P.I.

 

career:

Was employed in the Philippine National Museum at Manila from Aug. 1947 to Feb. 1951.

 

Collecting localities:

1947-51. Collected on Mt Pulog (Luzon, March 1948) with A.P. Castro, and > 300 plants in Mindanao (March-Nov. 1950) with K.J. Pelzer. Philippines. Luzon: Abra and Mountain Province; Palawan; Mindanao: Mt Apo, Cotabato; Basilan. 

 

collections:

Numbered in the P.N.H. series (cf. sub Philippine National Herbarium). In PNH Manila [PNH].
