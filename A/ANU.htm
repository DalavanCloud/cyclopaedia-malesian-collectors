InformatioN:

A series made by staff and students working under Dr Donald Walker in the Department of Geography of the Institute of Advanced Studies. Apart from Dr and Mrs (Pat) Walker*, collectors are: John Flenley*, Josselyn Wheeler*, Dr Donald MacVean*, and L. Keith Wade* in New Guinea, and Martin Kellman* in Mindanao (Philippines).
