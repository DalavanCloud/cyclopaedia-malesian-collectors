Amad, Raden Mas

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Surgeon, in 1909 attached to Abendanon’s Centr. Celebes Expedition;1 at the beginning engaged in collecting plants, assisted by sergeant J.J. Lefèvre; since May 20th till his departure he had to contend with illness.

 

Collecting localities:

cf. sub Abendanon (also for literature).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: some waterplants from the lakes Matana and Towoeti about Oct. 1909. He discovered the new endemic plant Boottia mesenterium Hallier f.2

 

literature:

(1) cf. E.C. Abendanon: ‘Midden-Celebes Expeditie’ vol. 1, 1915, p. 101.

(2) cf. Meded.’s Rijks Herb. Leiden no 26, 1915, p. 7.
