Aart, Johannes Hendrikus van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

a resident of Ambon, in 1885 sent a collection of dried Sideroxylon species to Herb. Bog. The species were thought identical with those described in the work of Rumphius. 

 

Collecting localities:

Ambon (Moluccas), 1885. Also many living orchids an myrmecophilous plants to Hort. Bog. (1885).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO].
