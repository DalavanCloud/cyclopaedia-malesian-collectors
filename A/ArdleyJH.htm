Ardley, John H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Entomologist with the Papua and New Guinea Department of Agriculture; subsequently Technical Adviser-Entomology with William Cooper & Nephews, PTY. Ltd. at Concord, N.S.W., Australia.

 

Collecting localities:

Mainly Sarawaket (New Guinea) plants associated with insect pests or food plants of insects.

 

collections:

Partly numbered serially under collector’s name, others without number; in Herb. Lae. [LAE]. New Zealand plants in Herb. Domin. Mus. Wellington.
