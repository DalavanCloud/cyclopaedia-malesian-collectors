Atje(h) = Rd. Atje Adiwidjaja

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Died: Maseng near Bogor, Indonesia.

 

career:

Indonesian, in the employ of the Botanic Garden Buitenzorg.

 

Collecting localities:

1913-14. With Expedition van Hulstijn (see there) to the Soela Islands. According to Versl. Pl. Tuin Buitenzorg for 1913, his departure from Buitenzorg took place on July 18, 1912; this is, apparently, a printer’s error and rightly should be 1913. Collecting was done in the following islands: Soela besi (= Sanana), Mangoli (e.g. on G. Pakao), Taliaboe, P. Masonie, P. Seho and P. Kano. During the latter part of the expedition van Hulstijn was assisted by Saänam (see there).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 420 nos Soela Islands Exp. van Hulstijn. Living plants in Hort. Bog.
