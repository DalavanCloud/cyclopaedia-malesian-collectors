Askey, J.E.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Ranger in the Forest Department (1905-10).

 

Collecting localities:

Collected in the Malay Peninsula (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).

 

collections:

Herb. Kuala Lump. [KEP], e.g. from Negri Sembilan, the Dindings, and mainly Perak and Penang; numbered in the C.F. (see sub Conservator of Forests) series.
