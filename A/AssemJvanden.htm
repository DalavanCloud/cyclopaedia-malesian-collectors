Assem, Johannes van den

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1930, Rotterdam, Netherlands

 

career:

Zoologist educated at Leiden University (M.S. 1954); from Apr. 1956-Apr. 1959 entomologist of the ‘Malaria-bestrijding’ of the Medical Service in New Guinea. Later on the staff of the Zoological Laboratory at Leiden. Ph.D. at Leiden (1967).

 

collections:

Some tens of weeds collected at Ifar, Hollandia, North New Guinea (Oct. 1956); in Herb. Manokwari [MAN] and Leiden [L].
