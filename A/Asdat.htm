Asda(t)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A Sundanese employed by the Herbarium at Buitenzorg.

 

Collecting localities:

He attended Bloembergen (see there) on his trip to the Soela Islands in 1939, and a soil scientific expedition under the direction of van der Voort and Tänzer (see those) of the Soil Science Institute (Buitenzorg), to the environs of Troemon in SW. Atjeh (N. Sumatra) (Aug. 17-Sept. 2, 1941).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 207 nos Sumatra plants; Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: 69 nos Soela Islands, numbered in the bb. series, collected by Asda & Anta.
