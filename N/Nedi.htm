career:

‘Mantri’ of the Herbarium at Buitenzorg, who attended G.A.L. de Haan (see there) on the first part of his stay in the Moluccas.

 

Collecting localities:

Moluccas, 1937. Batjan: Masoeroeng, G. Damar (200 m) (Aug. 11-13); Waringin, Kali Sajoeang and G. Kewasa (14-17); Laboeha (Aug. 19-Sept. 2); kp. Inggoi (near kali Inggoi) (3); Amasing (9-12); Halmaheira: Djailolo (Sept. 28-30); (So)-Soepoe (Oct. 1-3), kp. Taroeba, kp. Todahe, etc.; kp. Toegoeair (6); kp. Bigadoe (11); Djiko djira and Lebengon (12-13); Kane (16-19); kp. Koesoe (20-24); Obi: Laiwoei (Oct. 30-Dec. 5), collecting at kp. Boeton, kp. Baoe, kp. Doeri, kp. Djiko Dolong, kp. Boeloe, Telaga, Kali Kasna, G. Dihoeroe, G. Damar Manomang (Nov. 17-19), P. Bisa (Nov. 26-27); P. Santari (28); kp. Baroe (29); kp. Woei (Dec. 4-5); P. Tapa(t) (Dec. 8); Batjan: Laboeha (11).-1955. E. Borneo: Blajan River near Samarinda (March 21).-1967. Apr. 10 with Soegeng to New Guinea (Irian) to take part in the border expedition with Australian New Guinea.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: in total 714 nos, viz from Batjan nos 1-166, Halmaheira nos 167-470, Obi nos 471-628, P. Bisa nos 629-650, P. Santari 651-667, Obi 668-700, P. Tapa 701, Batjan 702-714.
