Nainggolan, F.J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

In 1931 a resident of Peureula(k), E. coast of Atjeh (N. Sumatra), and in 1937 of Medan (Sumatra East Coast).

Author of some papers dealing with animals, in ‘De Tropische Natuur’ for 1931 seq.

 

Collecting localities: & collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: some nos from Atjeh, N. Sumatra, collected in April 1931, at Djoeloe Rajeu, (B.P.M., sub-division of Idi), etc.
