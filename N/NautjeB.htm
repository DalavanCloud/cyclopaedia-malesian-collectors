Nautje, Barnabas

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1937, Kondjobandoe, South New Guinea.

 

career:

Attended the Bosbouwschool at Hollandia, N. New Guinea; onwards of 1957 in the employ of the Forestry Service.

 

Collecting localities:

1957. N. New Guinea: Hamadi near Hollandia (March 19, 28); S. New Guinea: Asmat area, at Erma, Sauwah, Komor (June 23-Aug. 6).

 

collections:

About 125 nos, numbered in the B.W. series, in Herb. Manokwari [MAN], dupl. at Leiden [L].
