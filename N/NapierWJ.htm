Napier, Sir Walter J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1857. Died: 1945.

 

career:

A lawyer of Singapore and Attorney General (1908-09); on the Singapore Gardens Committee (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]: plants from different places in the Malay Peninsula, collected at Taiping (190 .), etc.
