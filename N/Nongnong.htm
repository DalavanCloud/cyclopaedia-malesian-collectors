Nongnong

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A native collector employed by W.M. Docters van Leeuwen (see there); possibly identical with Nengneng (see there).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: plants from W. Java, from G. Tjisalak (Febr. 1922), and near Buitenzorg.

Hort. Bog.: living plants from the environs of Buitenzorg.
