Nolthenius, Lt

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Was temporarily stationed (Sept.-Oct. 1896, Dec. 1896-Jan. 1897) on Lang Island in Sunda Strait;1 he met Boerlage (see there), when the latter visited the island in Sept. 1896.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: small collection made in the 2nd half of 1896.
