collections:

Is cited as the collector of Gonna motleyana Pierre ex Dubard (cf. Lam in Bull. Jard. Bot. Buit. sér. 3, vol. 7, 1925, p. 123), at Bandjermasin in SE. Borneo; the cited specimen in Herb. Bog. [BO].
