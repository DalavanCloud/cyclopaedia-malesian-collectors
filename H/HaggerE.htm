Hagger, E.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Educated at Zürich (Switzerland), Forest Officer of the Forestry Bureau, Manila (see there), collected a few species in the island of Polillo in 1904.

He is commemorated in Ficus haggeri Merr.

 

collections:

Herb. Manila [PNH], including the type of Trigonachras cuspidata Radlk. from Polillo,1 and 10 plants from Tayabas Prov., Luzon (acq. 1903/04); U.S. Nat. Herb. Wash. [US]: 7 dupl. from Luzon. Numbered in the F.B. (see Forestry Bureau, Manila) series.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Robinson in Philip. Journ. Sci. C. Bot. 6, 1911, p. 186.
