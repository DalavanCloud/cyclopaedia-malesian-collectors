Collections

 

Hope

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is cited by Merrill in Enum. Born. Pl. 1921 as the collector of Arachnis lowei (Lindl.) Rchb.f. var. rohaniana (Rchb.f.) J.J.Sm. in Borneo.

We know nothing about his identity; it seems improbable that he should be identical with Charles Will. Webley Hope, of whom 29 ferns from North India are preserved in Herb. Kew [K] (pres. 1889); cf. also sub Hupe, as the name may have been misread. See also under Hope.
