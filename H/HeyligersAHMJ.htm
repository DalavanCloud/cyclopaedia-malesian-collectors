Heyligers, Arthur Henricus Maria Josephus

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1870, Antwerp, Belgium. Died: 1936, The Hague, Holland.

 

career:

Came to the D.E.I. in 1882; he joined the Civil Service in 1911 and was successively Administrator in: Timor and Dependencies (Lesser Sunda Islands), Palembang Res. (S. Sumatra) (1921-26), the Moluccas (1926-28), Manado Res. (N. Celebes) (1928-30), Billiton (1930-32). He was pensioned off early in 1932.

 

collections:

Living plants from Savoe (= Sawoe, Lesser Sunda Isls) to Hort. Bog. in 1917-19.
