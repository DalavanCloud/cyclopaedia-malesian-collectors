Horsburgh, Rev. H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

G.L. Jacob in his biography of Sir J. Brooke (see there) mentions a Reverend A. Horsburgh, for some years a missionary in Sarawak (before 1844) and in 1853 chaplain of the Bengal Establishment at Delhi; he may be identical with the above-mentioned.

 

collections:

Java plants of his were presented to Herb. Kew [K] in 1856.
