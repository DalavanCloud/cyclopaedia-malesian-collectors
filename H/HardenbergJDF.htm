Hardenberg, Johann Dietrich Frans

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1902, Willem I, Ambarawa, Java.

 

career:

Zoologist, educated at Utrecht University, where he took his Ph. Dr’s degree in 1927; in 1927 appointed at the Marine Biological Laboratory at Batavia, since 1933 Chief of that institution.

Author of many, especially ichthyological, papers.

 

Collecting localities:

GrootMassalemba (= Groot Salemboe) in the Java Sea (May 1930); Aroe Islands: P. Wamar, Dobo (1938).

 

collections:

A few nos in Herb. Bog. [BO], including Euphorbia heterophylla from Groot Salemboe, collected together with H.C. Delsman (see there).

 

biographical data:

Wie is dat? 1935, p. 455-456.
