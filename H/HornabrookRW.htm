Hornabrook, Richard William

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1925, Wellington, New Zealand.

 

career:

Neurologist (M.D. New Zealand 1956), Director of the Institute of Human Biology, Port Moresby, Papua. Clinical Investigator, Kuru Research, Eastern Highlands New Guinea, 1963-65.

Author of medical and entomological papers of Kuru etc. in various journals.

 

Collecting localities:

1963-65. New Guinea: Eastern Highlands, especially in Okapa subdistrict.

 

collections:

In Herb. Lae [LAE].
