Heyting, L.C.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1890, Padangsidimpoean, Tapanoeli, Sumatra.

 

career:

Entered the D.E. Indian Civil Service in 1917; District Officer in SE. Borneo (1925), Sumatra East Coast (1925-28); District Officer and later Assistant Resident in Bali & Lombok Res. (1928-32, stationed in Soemba etc.); Assistant Resident at Sintang, W. Borneo (1933-34); retired in 1935.

 

Collecting localities:

Bali: G. Tabanan (1925); W. Borneo: G. K(e)lam (Oct. 1933, Apr. 1934).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: a few nos, including Rafflesia from Borneo.
