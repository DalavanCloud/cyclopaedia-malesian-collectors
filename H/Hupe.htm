Hupe

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Is cited as the collector of Oleandra tricholepis Kuntze.1 Lycopodium hupeanum Mull. was named after him.2

A certain C. Hupe is the author of a paper on the religion, customs, etc. of the Dyaks.3 The latter was a German missionary, as was the collector; cf. also sub Hope.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Bot. Zeitung 9, 1851, p. 349.

(2) cf. l.c. 19, 1861, p. 165.

(3) cf. Tijdschr. Neêrl. Ind. 83, 1846, p. 127-172, 245-280.
