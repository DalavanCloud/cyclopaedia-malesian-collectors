Henar, G.H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Since April 1930 in temporary employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service; first stationed at Samarinda (E. Borneo) and since May 1937 at Kota Baroe (SE. Borneo), working in P. Lacet, Tanah-Boemboe, etc. In April 1938 he quitted Government service and entered provincial service, he was appointed Overseer of Forests in Boeloengan, stationed at Noenoekan, NE. Borneo.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: nos 52, 55-64 and 67-71 from P. Makijan (SE. Borneo) (Febr. 1936), presented through the Forest Research Institute (F.R.I.) at Buitenzorg + other dupl. F.R.I. In Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: from E. Borneo, P. Laoet, P. Tarakan, P. Mandoel, P. Noenoekan, and NE. Borneo, several hundreds of bb. numbers.
