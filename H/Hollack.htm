Hollack, Captain

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Probably one of the members (military detachment?) of the Sepik Expedition, 1912-13 (cf. sub Ledermann for detailed itiner., liter., etc.).

 

Collecting localities:

He collected Lycopodium cernuum in Malu bivouac, former Kaiser- Wilhelmsland (= NE. New Guinea) (Apr. 1912).1
