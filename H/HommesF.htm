Hommes, F.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: Drowned during World War II on a transport of P.O.W.’s.

 

career:

Manager of the s.f. Gondangwinangoen, subsequently taking over the management of a lime-kiln at Kates, Blitar (E. Java); on European furlough and after his return in 1939 manager of the s.f. Kremboong. In May 1940 interned on account of his political opinions.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 6 nos from Djokja and Soerakarta in Centr. Java (coll. 1926).
