Hose, Gertrude

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1883, Sarawak.

 

career:

From 1883-88 and 1903-08 in Sarawak with her father Bishop Hose. from 1923-25 in Malaya with her brother Edw. S. Hose (C.F. Cowan in litt. Feb. 12, 1968).

 

Collecting localities:

In various parts of the Malay Peninsula,1 e.g. about Taiping town with Bishop Hose in March 1904.-NW Borneo, Sarawak (about 1903).

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]: Bornean grasses (pres. 1903)2 and probably others with those of G.Fr. Hose; Herb. Sarawak [SAR]: grasses and sedges coll. with G.Fr. Hose (see there).

 

literature:

(1) cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5.

(2) Described by Ridley together with those of Bishop Hose in Journ. Str. Br. Roy As. Soc. no 46, 1906, p. 215-228.
