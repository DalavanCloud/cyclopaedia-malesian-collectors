career + collections:

Bauhinia from P. Penang in Herb. Copenhagen [C].

Mentioned as collector in the Addenda, Cyclopaedia of Collectors (Fl. Mal. 1) p. 604, did not collect himself in Malaysia, but the plant mentioned was from Herb. Hornemann (= Copenhagen [C]), owned formerly by Jens Wilken Hornemann (1770-1841), editor of the ‘Flora Danica’, who from 1801 was lecturer in botany at Copenhagen University, and since 1817 professor there.
