Houten, Hendrik Cornelis van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Since 1872 Assistant Resident in the D.E. Indian Civil Service, stationed in Borneo’s Western Division.

 

collections:

In the years 1872-74 he forwarded plants to Hort. Bog. from Sambas, W. Borneo. He evidently had them collected.1
