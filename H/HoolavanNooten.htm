Career

 

Hoola van Nooten, Mrs

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A resident of Batavia, who sent cultivated plants to Hort. Bog. in the years 1872-73.

She is probably identical with Mrs Bertha Hoola van Nooten-van Dolder, authoress and paintress of ‘Fleurs, fruits et feuillages choisis de la flore et de la pomone de file de Java, peints d’après nature’ (Bruxelles 1863, 36 tab. col.).
