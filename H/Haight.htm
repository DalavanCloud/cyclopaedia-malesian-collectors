Haight

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

An American ex-soldier who contracted tuberculosis in the Philippines, and then settled in the mountains of Luzon, at Pauai (N. part of Benguet Prov.), growing vegetables. He collected orchids and a few other plants in Luzon; Merrill, Enum. Philip. Fl.Pl., mentioned the nos 10, 14 and 127.

 

collections:

Orchids in O. Ames Herb., Cambridge (Mass.) [GH], and probably originally in Herb. Manila [PNH] (now destroyed).
