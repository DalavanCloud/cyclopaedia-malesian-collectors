Hardenberg, Coenraad Cornelis

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1822, Gouda, Z.H., Holland. Died: 1864, Makassar, Celebes.

 

career:

Joined the D. E. Indian Civil Service in 1843; at first stationed in Java, in 1851 transferred to W. Borneo, since 1855 as Assistant Resident at Sambas; from 1859 till his death Assistant Resident at Makassar, SW. Celebes.

 

collections:

From W. Borneo he forwarded some dried plants to the ‘Natuurkundige Vereeniging’ at Batavia (1858), from where they were sent to Teysmann, Herb. Bog. [BO], for identification.1

 

literature:

(1) C.C. Hardenberg & P.F.H. Fromberg: ‘Over eenige planten van Westelijk Borneo met betrekking tot de papierbereiding’ (Nat. Tijdschr. N.I. 11, 1856, p. 295-302).
