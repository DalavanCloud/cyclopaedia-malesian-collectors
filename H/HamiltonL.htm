Hamilton, Miss L.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Now Mrs Read at Lae; in 1956 stationed at Okapa, Eastern Highlands, New Guinea, as a Nutritionist with Public Health Department.

 

collections:

About 230 nos (no good specimens); in Herb. Lae [LAE], and sent to various institutions for identification by specialists, to Leiden in 1958. Mainly useful plants from Papua gardens; partly with C nos, partly in the NGF series.
