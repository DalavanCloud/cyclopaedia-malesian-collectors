Collections

 

Hulshoff Pol

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

A resident of Buitenzorg, presented living orchids from Sumatra and Borneo, viz from Koetei (E. Borneo), from Oeloe Limau Manis (Sumatra West Coast) and from Moeara Tembesi (Djambi) to Hort. Bog. Some are described by J.J. Smith and preserved in Herb. Bog. [BO].
