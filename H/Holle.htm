Holle

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

A certain Holle, without initials, is cited several times as the collector of liverworts and mosses.1 We do not know whether he is identical with K.F. Holle. In the list of contributors to Leiden Herbarium [L] a Holle with the initials D.G. is mentioned as a collector of cryptogams; the latter collected phanerogams too.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Miquel, Plantae Junghuhnianae, and Verdoorn in Ann. Bryol. Suppl. vol. 4, 1934 (= Studien über Asiatische Jubuleae, Thesis, Utrecht 1934).
