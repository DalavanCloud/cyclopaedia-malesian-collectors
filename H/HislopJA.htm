Hislop, J.A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1912.

 

career:

Arrived in Malaya September 1, 1948; in 1953 Game Warden for East Region, Pahang. He took the probationer gardener’s course at Edinburgh, Royal Botanic Garden.1

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]: plants from G.. Padang (4000 ft) in Trengganu, Malay Peninsula in July 1952, and some small odds and ends.

 

literature:

(1) Author of: ‘Journey to Ulu Tekai’ (Mal. For. 19, 1955, p. 64-70).
