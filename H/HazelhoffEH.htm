Hazelhoff, Engel Hendrik

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1900, Blijham, Gr., Holland. Died: 1945, Groningen, Holland.

 

career:

Zoologist who was educated and took his Dr’s degree (1926) at Utrecht University; teacher at secondary schools, 1923-26, successively at Woerden and Rotterdam; Entomologist of the Experiment Station for the Java Sugar Industry at Pasoeroean (E. Java), 1926-31; subsequently appointed Professor of Zoology in the University of Groningen, Holland.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 1 no from sf. Kedawoeng, Pasoeroean, E. Java (1928).

 

biographical data:

Vakbl. Biologen 25, 1945, p. 97-98.
