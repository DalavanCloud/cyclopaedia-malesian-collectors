Harmsen, Gerrit Johan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1890, Zutfen, Gld, Holland. Died: 1940, Eefde, Gld, Holland.

 

career:

Horticultural Assistant, since 1916 in D.E. Indian Government service, stationed successively at Makassar (SW. Celebes) 1916-21, Buitenzorg (W. Java) 1921--23, Ragoenan (W. Java) 1924, and at Poenten (near Malang, E. Java), Nov. 1924-1931. In 1931 he retired and went to Holland.

 

Collecting localities and collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 3 Loranthaceae collected in subdistrict of Sisir, E. Java (1928).
