Heering

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

No further data are known to us; he may be identical with P. Heering, a clergyman, author of ‘Indische schetsen’ (Leiden 1886, 2nd ed. 1897). The latter ascended G. Tjeremai (2nd half of Sept. 1871), G. Tengger (from the Pasoeroean side), G. Ardjoeno-Welirang via Lalidjiwo, G. Smeroe (Ranoe Koembolo and Ranoe Panic) and back to Watès, and visited Lawang. On l.c. p. 27 he mentions the collecting of some plant souvenirs; he sometimes refers to Junghuhn.

 

collections:

Collected Ageratum conyzoides L. at Tjimahi, W. Java, preserved in Herb. Bog. [BO].
