Hiemstra (van Gelderen), J.L.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Author of some typed reports (1927) on the mangrove forests in Langkat, Administrator of the Forest Service at Langkat, Sumatra East Coast.

 

collections:

Presented dried material of a number of mangrove species to the Herb. Kol. (= Ind.) Inst. Amsterdam, in 1927. In Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: from Sumatra East Coast, collected in 1922.
