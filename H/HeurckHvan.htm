Collections

 

Heurck, Dr Henri van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is cited as the collector of some plants in the Lampong Districts, S. Sumatra, e.g. of Argyreia capitata (Vahl) Choisy no 8 in Febr. 1934 (cf. v. Ooststroom in Blumea 5, 1943, p. 369). Material in Herb. Bog. [BO].

The collector is certainly not the well-known Henri van Heurck from Antwerp, who died in 1909.
