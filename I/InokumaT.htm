Born: 1904, Ehimeken, Japan.

 

career:

Graduate of Tokyo Imp. University (faculty of Forestry); Assistant Professor of Botany at Tokyo Imp. University, who visited Java in Febr. 1939, on the way to New Guinea for the investigation of forest trees. At present Professor.

 

Collecting localities:

1939. Between March 25-May 10 in Dutch West New Guinea, Vogelkop: at Mo(e)mi; in Dutch N. New Guinea, on the Geelvink Bay: at Nabire, Sennen, Chaban, Papaja, Patema, Dallmann, Bumi (= Boeme) River.-1943. Dutch New Guinea.

 

collections:

Herb. Forestry Inst. Tokyo Imp. Univ. [TOFO]: > 755 nos, collected with K. Hara.1 Duplicates were promised to Herb. Bog. [BO].

 

literature:

(1) R. Kanehira: ‘A summary of our knowledge of Papuan Pandanus’ (Bot. Magaz. Tokyo 54, 1940, p. 249-260, 9 fig.).

S. Kubata: ‘A new species of Vavaea in Netherland North New Guinea’ (Bull. Tokyo Univ. Forests no 35, 1947, p. 198-199, 1 pl.); ‘An enumeration of Verbenaceae collected by Prof. T. Inokuma and K. Hara in Netherland North New Guinea’ (l.c. p. 202-206, pl. 2).

S. Kubata: ‘Rutaceae collected by Prof. T. Inokuma and K. Hara in Netherland North New Guinea’ (Bull. Tokyo Univ. Forests no 36, Dec. 1948, p. 124-129, 2 pl.); ‘A new species of Ternstroemia in Netherland North New Guinea’ (l.c. p. 132-134, 1 pl.).
