Ijiri, Shôji

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1913, Otaru city, Hokkaido, Japan.

 

career:

Graduated from the University of Tokyo (1936), specialist in Palaeontology and Odontology of Mammals; Dr. Sc. Tokyo (1949).

 

Collecting localities:

West New Guinea from March to June 1943, with the Satake (see there) party.

 

collections:

About 700 sheets of flowering plants; in Herb. Nat. Sc. Mus. Tokyo [TNS].
