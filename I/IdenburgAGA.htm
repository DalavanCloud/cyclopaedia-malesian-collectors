Born: 1904, Utrecht, Holland. Died: June 23, 1949, by plane crash, Bari, Italy.

 

career:

Pedologist, educated at Wageningen Agricultural College; in 1930 appointed at the Soil Science Institute, Buitenzorg; Dr’s degree at Wageningen in 1937;1 from 1939 onwards in Holland, first on sick-leave and subsequently on half-pay.

 

Collecting localities:

1931. S. Sumatra, Lampong Distr.: Menggala (May), from Pelaboean Soengai Badak to Oemboelan Garoenggoeng, Sg. Gebang bivouac.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 61 nos.

 

literature:

(1) Author of ‘Systematische grondkarteering van Zuid-Sumatra’ (Thesis, Wageningen 1937).
