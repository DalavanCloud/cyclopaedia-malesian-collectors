Iterson, Gerrit van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1878, Roermond, L., Holland. Died: 1972, Wassenaar, ZH., Holland.

 

career:

Was educated at the Technical College at Delft; Chief Assistant of Microbiology, 1901-07; in 1907 appointed Professor of Technical Botany. In 1911 he came to Java as a delegate of the Dutch Government to the Fibre Congress, but spent some time at Buitenzorg for research work of a purely botanical nature in August.1

 

collections:

A collection of economic plants and their products (coll. 1911) in Techn. Univ. Coll. Delft.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Dammerman in Ann. Jard. Bot. Buit. 45, 1935, p. 36.

 

biographical data:
