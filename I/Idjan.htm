Idjan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Indonesian, in 1942 for about 7 years in the employ of the Herbarium at Buitenzorg; assistant of Aët (see there) on the van Dijk Expedition (see there) to P. Japen and Biak, N of New Guinea.

 

Collecting localities:

1939. P. Biak (Sept. 20-30); Aët, at that time being ill, stayed behind in P. Japen.-In Ternate and Halmaheira he made a collection with Mochtar bin Musa in 1951, viz 399 nos of which part was in a bad state and not preserved (for itinerary cf. sub D.R. Pleyte).-In 1953 he accompanied an expedition to Sumatra West Coast (itinerary cf. sub van Borssum Waalkes).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: nos 846-955 (numbered in Aët’s series), van Dijk Exp.

Ternate and Halmaheira collections in Herb. Bog. [BO]. Sumatra West Coast: to my knowledge all collected plants were incorporated in the number series of van Borssum Waalkes.
