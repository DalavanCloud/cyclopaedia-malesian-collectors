Vriens, de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Probably the collector rightly was Dr J.G.C. Vriens, Head of the Research Laboratory for Deli Tobacco of the Agric. Department in the D.E.I. in 1905.

 

collections:

Is cited by Martelli as the collector of Pandanus vriensii Martelli n.sp. at Bandai Barai, G. Sibajak in Jan. 1905 (cf. Webbia, 4, 1913, p. 434). In Herb. Martelli (= Florence [FI]).
