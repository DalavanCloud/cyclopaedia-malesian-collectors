Vos, Huguette Constance Cornelia Adriana Abdia

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1898, Breda, N.Br., Holland.

 

career:

Biologist who studied and took her Ph.Dr’s degree (1930) at Utrecht University. Subsequently she came to Java where she held various appointments as a teacher. Since 1939 stationed at Semarang. After internment by the Japanese, and evacuation to Holland, she returned to Indonesia in May 1947.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: some plants from Pasir Nangka (Tjibeber, W. Java, June 1932 coll.), and from Soebah (Centr. Java, 1939).
