Ven, Leendert Albert van der

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1872, Probolinggo, Java.

 

career:

Forest Officer who entered the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service in 1897 and retired in 1932. During his term of office he was stationed in several places in Java.

 

collections:

He collected some plants numbered in Beumée’s series (see there), e.g. Cinnamomum iners Reinw., no 6616, at S. Batjem in Kediri Res., E. Java (Nov. 21, 1923).
