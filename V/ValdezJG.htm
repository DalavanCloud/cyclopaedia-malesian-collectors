Valdez, Juan Gonzalez

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

First Forest Inspector in the Philippines; he collected material of forest trees.1 The ‘Inspection General de Montes’ was erected during Spanish régime in 1865.2

 

collections:

Herb. de la escuela especial de montes, Escorial; according to Vidal much material without flowers or fruits.1

 

literature:
