Vonk, Hendrik Willem

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1897, Den Helder, N.H., Holland.

 

career:

Joined the D.E. Indian Civil Service in 1919; in 1946/47 at the disposal of the delegate for Indonesian Affairs at The Hague; retired in 1947.

 

collections:

When Assistant Resident of Boeton (= Boetoeng, S of Celebes), he sent a specimen of Vandopsis to Hort. Bog. in 1937.
