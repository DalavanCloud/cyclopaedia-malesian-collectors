Vogel, C.J. de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Coffee planter, possibly in 1904 Overseer at Tjipetir (Govt Gutta-percha Estate), W. Java.

 

Collecting localities:

E. Java: Kandangan (1895-96), Djember (1897), Pasirian (1899); S. Sumatra, Lampong Districts: Negara Ratoe Estate (1915, and March 15, 1920); W. Sumatra, Tapanoeli: Padang Sidempoean (1917).

 

collections:

Herb. Kol. (= Ind.) Inst. Amsterdam (former Kol. Mus. Haarlem): coffee varieties, weeds from coffee plantations, and officinal plants from Java (above-mentioned localities); Herb. Bog. [BO]: Sumatra plants.
