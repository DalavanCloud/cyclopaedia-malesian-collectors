Venning, Alfred Reid

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: 1927.

 

career:

In the Malayan Civil Service from 1893-1908.

 

Collecting localities:

When Secretary of the Resident, Perak (1900-1903), residing at Taiping, he collected some plants in 1901 or 1902.

 

collections:

Material in Herb. Sing. [SING] (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).
