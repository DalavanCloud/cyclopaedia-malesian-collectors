Veenhuizen

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

? Possibly A.C. Veenhuyzen, Resident of Riouw in about 1914.

 

Collecting localities:

Is cited as the collector of Selaginella longiaristata Hieron., no la, ‘Used as medicine’, in Billiton (cf. Alston in Bull. Jard. Bot. Buit. sér. 3., vol. 14, 1937, p. 178).

 

collections:

Material of the cited specimen in Herb. Bog. [BO] and Herb. Leiden [L].
