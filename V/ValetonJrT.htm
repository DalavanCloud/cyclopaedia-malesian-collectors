Valeton Jr, Théodore

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1881, Amsterdam, Holland. Died: early 1962 or late 1961, Netherlands.

 

career:

Went to Java in 1908, from 1924-34 Director of the School of Agriculture at Buitenzorg, and subsequently retired.

 

collections:

In Herb. Bog. [BO]: some 23 nos from Tjadasmalang, W. Java (Sept. 11, 1917), etc.
