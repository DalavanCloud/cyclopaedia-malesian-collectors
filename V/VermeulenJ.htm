Vermeulen, Dr J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Chemist, from 1919-27 teacher at the K.W. III School, Batavia, Java; later for some time employed at the Laboratory for Chemical Analysis, Buitenzorg.

 

Collecting localities:

W. Java: Cheribon Residency (1920) on the slopes of G. Tjeremai, collecting at Madja (May 3l), Argalinga, Telaga ramis (Mandirantjang Distr.), etc.; Bali: at Kintamani (June 4, 1921), etc.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: > 66 nos.
