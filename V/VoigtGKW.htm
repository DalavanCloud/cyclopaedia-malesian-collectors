Voigt, Dr G.K.W.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

On the staff of the ‘Biologische Reichsanstalt für Land- and Forstwirtschaft, Filiale Naumburg/Saale’ for applied chemistry and biology. 

 

collections:

During a 4-month stay at Batavia (W. Java), from end 1934-35, he collected diseased, introduced, and pasture plants, on behalf of some German collectors. The plants were intended for somebody of the staff of the above-mentioned institution.
