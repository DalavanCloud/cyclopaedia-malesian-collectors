Baumhauer, Jan Willem Samuel

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Doctor of Law, Assistant Resident at Montrado, W. Borneo.

 

Collecting localities:

Possibly he collected in the Lingga Archipelago in 1872.

 

collections:

Sent living plants to Hort. Bog. in 1866 and to Teysmann (Herb. Bog. [BO]) in 1868 (cf. Nat. Tijdschr. N.I. 30, 1868, p. 462). [Note C.G.G.J. van Steenis: in 1872 in Zuiggaard coll.]
