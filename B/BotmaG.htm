Botma, Geertje

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1921, Boerakker, Fr., Holland.

 

career:

Schoolteacher and nurse who after World War II went to Indonesia for the Red Cross; at present married to Dr V. de Bruyn, official of the Civil Service in New Guinea.

 

Collecting localities:

1947. Java; Lombok (Lesser Sunda Isls); Biak (Schouten Isls). 1949. Biak.

 

collections:

50 Plants in Herb. D.T.E. van der Ploeg (Bergum, Fr., Holland) [now in L], including 2 from Java and 1 from Lombok. Some dupl. in Herb. Leiden [L].
