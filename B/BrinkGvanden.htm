collections:

Herb. Leiden [L]: plants from the Malaysian Archipelago and China, e.g. from Java. The herbarium of Balansa at Leiden contains a specimen of Erianthus velutinus collected by G. van den Brink (the name printed on a card). On the label is written Java in the same hand as the plant name. As this very rare species is known only from Further India, Khasia, etc., the locality ‘Java’ seems very doubtful.
