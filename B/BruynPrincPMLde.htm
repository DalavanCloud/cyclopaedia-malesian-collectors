Bruyn Prince, Pieter Merkus Lambertus de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1851, Soerabaja, Java. Died: 1934, Voorburg, Z.H., Holland.

 

career:

Since 1873 official in the D.E. Indian Civil Service; from 1896 Resident of Kedoe, Centr. Java; in 1901 elected a Member of Council, in 1904 Vice-president.

 

collections:

At the request of Herb. Bog. [BO], he sent dried material of Alphonsea javanica Scheff. from Kedoe in Centr. Java (cf. Icon. Bogor. 1, fast. 2, 1899, p. 114).
