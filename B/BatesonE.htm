Bateson, Mrs E.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Wife of a Government Mycologist who retired about 1930.

 

Collecting localities:

She collected plants for Dr E.D. Merrill in Br. N. Borneo, e.g. a no 59 = Homonoia riparia Lour. near Maruda Bay (June 1923) (cf. Merrill in Philip. Journ. Sci. 30, 1926, p. 81).
