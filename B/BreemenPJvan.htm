Breemen, Pieter Johan van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1875, The Hague, Holland.

 

career:

A biologist, educated at Amsterdam University (Ph. Dr 1905); for 12 years employed in Curaçao at the Inspection of Fisheries as a planter, and at the Inspection of Agriculture (until 1920) ; Chief of the Field Experiment Service of the Experiment Station for Java Sugar Industry (Pasoeroean), 1921-27, at the same time Entomologist (till 1926). He subsequently retired and settled at The Hague.

 

Collecting localities:

1925. E. Java: G. Tengger (April, May), e.g. at Tosari, on G. Bromo, at Nongkodjadjar.-1926. G. Tengger, G. Bromo, Pasoeroean, Nongkodjadjar, Lawang, Poedjon, Malang Plain.-1927. W. Java: Priangan Residency.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 59 nos; dupl. in Herb. Bog. [BO].
