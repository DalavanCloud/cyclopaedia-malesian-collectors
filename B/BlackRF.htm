Black, Reverend R.F.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Addenda & Corrigenda)

 

career:

He is commemorated in Hetaeria blackii Ames.

 

collections:

Is cited as the collector of the nos 8, 10, 15, and 16 in Mindanao (cf. Merrill, Enum. Philip. Fl. Pl.); O. Ames (Orchidaceae fasc. 2, 1908) cites the nos 20, 22, 25, 27, all collected in 1906 at Davao, Mindanao, and some s.n. collected in Oct. 1905.
