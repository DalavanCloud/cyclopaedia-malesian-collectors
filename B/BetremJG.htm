Betrem, Johan George

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1899, The Hague, Holland.

 

career:

Zoologist, educated at Leiden University (Ph. Dr 1928); Assistant in the Entomological Laboratory at Wageningen, 1926-28, and in 1929 Phytopathologist; Entomologist of the Malang Experiment Station in E. Java, 1930-38, subsequently Agriculturist-Entomologist, and finally Head of the Central Java division. After World War II he was evacuated to Holland; at present teacher at the Colonial Agricultural School at Deventer.

Author of several zoogeographical, entomological, phytopathological, and eco-climatological studies.

 

Collecting localities:

E.g. Centr. Java: G. Moeriah, Djolong Estate (June 1939).

 

collections:

Herb. Leiden [L] and Herb. Bog. [BO], few plants.
