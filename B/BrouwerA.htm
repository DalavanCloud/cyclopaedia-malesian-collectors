Brouwer, Adolf

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

On monthly pay in the employ of the Forestry Service at Hollandia, N. New Guinea.

 

Collecting localities:

West New Guinea: in the sample plots near Holtekang pr. Hollandia; in 1955 near Oransbari, S of Manokwari.

 

collections:

Numbered in the B.W. series (cf. Boswezen Nieuw Guinea): in Herb. Leiden [L] and Manokwari [MAN]; in 1957 ± 250 nos.
