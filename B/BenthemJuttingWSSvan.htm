Born: 1899, Batavia, Java.

see also sub Feen-Van Benthem Jutting, W.S.S. van der.

 

career:

Malaco-zoologist, a schoolteacher who studied natural history at Amsterdam from 1918-24; in 1920 appointed Assistant, later Curator of the Zoological Museum, Amsterdam; Febr. 1930-April 1932 temporarily stationed at the Buitenzorg Museum specially charged with the identifying of the malaco-zoological collection; after return in Europe in her old position again. In 1945 she married P.J. van der Feen, biologist, but is still on the staff of the Museum.

 

Collecting localities:

Balanophora globosa from G. Wajang, Priangan Res. in W. Java (Febr. 22, 1931).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]; occasionally Fungi for Herb. Bog. [BO].
