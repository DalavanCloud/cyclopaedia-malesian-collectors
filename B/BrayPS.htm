Career

 

Bray, P.S.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

A British graduate volunteer under the Voluntary Service Overseas Programme; he graduated in 1968 with an external London degree in botany from Portsmouth Technical College. At present (1969) selling computers. Attached to the Forest Department Kepong from Sept. 1968-Mar. 1969. He occasionally collected in the series of the Forest Research Institute with P.F. Cockburn and maybe others in Malaya.
