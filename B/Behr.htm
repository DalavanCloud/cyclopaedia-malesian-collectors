Behr

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Is mentioned by Merrill as cited in literature as a collector of plants in the Philippines (cf. Bull. Bur. of Agr. Manila no 4, 1903, p. 30).

 

collections:

Herb. Utrecht [U]: plants of Dr Beckl from the Philippine Islands. As this name is unknown to us, it is supposed that the collector is Behr, and not Beckl.
