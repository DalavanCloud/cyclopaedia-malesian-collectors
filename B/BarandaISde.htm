Baranda, Isidro Sainz de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Mostly from S. parts of Luzon, also from Isl. Masbate, Isl. Baton (E of Luzon), about 1842.

 

collections:

Herb. Forestry School Madrid.1 

 

biographical data:

(1) D. Máximo Laguna y Villanueva: ‘Cien helechos de Filipinas despuestos con arreglo á la último edición (1874) de la Sinopsis Filicum de Hooker y Baker’ (Anal. Soc. Esp. Hist. Nat. 7, 1879, 19 pp.). He describes 102 species of ferns collected in the Philippines by Sainz de Baranda.
