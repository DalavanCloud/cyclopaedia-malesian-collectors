Bogen, Emil

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Commander of the American Marines. He is reported to have worked on a full account of the Admiralty Islands, including its natural history.

 

Collecting localities:

200 very fragmentary specimens from Manus Island in the Admiralties, collected during World War II in the forties of this century [= 1940’s].

 

collections:

U.S. Nat. Herb. Wash. [US].
