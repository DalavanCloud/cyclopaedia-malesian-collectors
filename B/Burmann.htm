Burman(n)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Is cited by Alston1 as the collector of Selaginella opaca Warb. near Semarang, Centr. Java.

 

collections:

The collections of Johannes and Nic. Laurens Burman are in Herb. Delessert, Geneva [G]; in Herb. Leiden [L]: plants of Johannes from Ceylon; in Herb. Munich [M]: plants of Nic. Laurens. By Decandolle2 no plants from the D.E.I. are mentioned. The herbarium Burman contains plants of Kleinhoff, Houttuyn, Pryon, van Royen, etc.3 We may safely assume that the above-mentioned plant was not collected by Burman himself, but was ex Herb. Burman.
