Brummeler, G.W. ten

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Chief of the Postal Service at Batavia, Java.

 

Collecting localities:

Had a private collection from Pajoekombo, (Sumatra West Coast), in which Burck (see there) came across many gutta-percha samples of perfect quality. Lam (in Bull. Jard. But. Buit. sér. 3, vol. 7, 1925, p. 135) refers to Payena dantung H.J.Lam, no 43, collected by a ten Brummeler at Ogan Hoeloe, Sum. West Coast.

 

collections:

Probably in Herb. Bog. [BO].
