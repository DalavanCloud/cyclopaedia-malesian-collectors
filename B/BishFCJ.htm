Bish, Freddy Charles Joagim

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1908, Weltevreden, Java.

 

career:

Overseer employed by the D.E. Indian Forest Service since the end of 1930; stationed in N. Celebes, respectively at Manado, Gorontalo, Donggala, and Manado; since 1935 in SW. Celebes at Rantapaoe and Watampone (1937); since June 1938 employed in Java.

 

Collecting localities:

Centr. Celebes: in the subdivisions Donggala (Manado Res.) and Paloe (Manado Res.) (1932-34); SW. Celebes (1935-37).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 3 nos of dupl. from Donggala (56, 58 and 60) and 10 nos from Paloe (nos betw. 61-74) (pres. through the intermediary of the Forest Research Inst.); Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: from Centr. Celebes (126 nos) and SW. Celebes.
