Booberg, Karl Gunnar

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1892, Orebro, Sweden. Died: Oct. 28, 1944, Tjimahi, Java.

 

career:

Peat expert and plant geographer who took his degree at Uppsala in 1930; till 1924 he was employed in Sweden, at first as Assistant in the Geological Service at Stockholm, subsequently by the ‘Svenska Mosskulturföreningen’, Jönköping; since 1925 employed at the Java Sugar Experiment Station (Pasoeroean), respectively Group-Adviser at Modjokerto 1925-26, Inspector of the Field Service 1926-28, Asst Director of the Agricultural Department 1928-34 and Director of the same Department 1934-41. After his retirement he settled at Djoenggo, Poenten (E. Java).

He is the author of many papers, principally concerning cane-breeding, but of some plant-sociological publications on the Malaysian flora too.1

 

Collecting localities:

1925. E. Java: Poeger.-1926. Poeger.-1927. Sumatra East Coast.-1928. E. Java: Poeger (with Backer).-1929. G. Watangan, Poeger.-1930. Centr. Java: Watoe Oeloe, Koedoes, Rembang-1931. W. Java: Indramajoe, Cheribon.-1934. Islands in the Bay of Bantam and Batavia.-1936. E. Java: at Bondowoso with Clason (see there) (Sept. 6).-1940. Centr. Java: Solo.

Collecting was done too in S. Priangan, W. Java, and at Gradjagan (SE. Java).

 

collections:

Herb. Uppsala [UPS]: 500 dune plants (pres. 1928); Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 68 nos, and Booberg & Backer 24 nos; Herb. Bog. [BO]: e.g. 19 specim. Clason & Booberg, without numbers. The specimens preserved at Pasoeroean and Buitenzorg are for the greater part without dates. Also some sheets in Herb. Nat. Hist. Mus. Stockholm [S]. Also some dupl. in Herb. Lund [LD].
