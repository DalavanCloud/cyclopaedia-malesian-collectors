Belgrave, T.B.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

An Australian physician who was interested in the scientific exploration of New Guinea, in which island he collected about 1887.

Eupomatia belgraveana F.Muell. was named after him.

 

collections:

Herb. Kew [K] and Melbourne [MEL].
