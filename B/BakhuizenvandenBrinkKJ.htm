Bakhuizen van den Brink, Karel Johan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1918, Buitenzorg, Java.

Died: Feb. 28, 1957, on board, off the Cocos Islands, on the voyage to Holland.

 

career:

Younger brother of R.C. Bakhuizen van den Brink Jr (see there), lastly Assistant on a tea estate S of Buitenzorg.

 

Collecting localities:

He is mentioned on the labels as the collector of Cyperaceae, etc., in total > 74 nos in the years 1922-23, evidently in the wake of his father.

 

collections:

Material in Herb. Bog. [BO].
