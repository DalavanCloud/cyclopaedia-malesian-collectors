Blow, Thomas Bates

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Collecting localities:

Visited Singapore in 1904 and collected Characeae (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).

 

collections:

Herb. Kew [K]: from the West Indies (Nitella) (pres. 1898); also later collections. In Herb. Brit. Mus. [BM] (with Herb. Groves): Charophyta.
