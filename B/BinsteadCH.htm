Binstead, Charles Herbert

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Reverend, British bryologist. 

 

Collecting localities:

He paid a visit to Penang and Singapore in 1913 (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5) and previously or subsequently visited Brit. N. Borneo (April-May),1 especially the coastal region of the NE. projection of the island and part about Tenom.

 

collections:

Mosses only? In Herb. Kew [K]. Types probably with Herb. Dixon in Herb. Brit. Mus. [BM].

 

literature:

(1) H.N. Dixon: ‘On a collection of Bornean mosses made by the Rev. C.H. Binstead’ (Journ. Linn. Soc. Lond. 1916, 32 pp. 2 pl.).
