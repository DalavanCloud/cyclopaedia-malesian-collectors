Baim, T.C.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Made plant collections on Samboga River, near Buna, NE. New Guinea, on Oct. 8, 1944, during World War II; in 1945 he collected at Hollandia, Dutch N. New Guinea.

 

collections:

Few specimens in Herb. Arn. Arbor. [A].
