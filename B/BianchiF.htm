Bianchi, Frans

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1918, Buitenzorg, Java.

 

career:

Botanist who studied at Amsterdam University. Since Sept. 1946 working in the Hugo de Vries Laboratory, Amsterdam University; taking his Ph.D. on a genetical subject there, and from 1964 Professor of Botany.

 

Collecting localities:

In August 1938 he collected some 300 nos on Mt Gedeh-Pangrango in W. Java.

 

collections:

In Herb. Univ. Amsterdam [AMD].
