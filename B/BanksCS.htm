Born: 1875, Washington, D.C., U.S.A.

 

career:

Since 1902 entomologist, Chief Division of Entomology, Bur. of Sci., Manila, P.I.

He is commemorated in Hedyotis banksii Elmer.

 

Collecting localities:

In 1902 he was sent to Negros for 4 months.

 

collections:

Collected in the B(ur). S(ci). series for Herb. Manila [PNH].
