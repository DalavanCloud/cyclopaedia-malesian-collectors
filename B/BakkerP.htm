Bakker, Pr.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A resident of Malang; about 1917 Assistant at Soebanajam Estate, Benkoelen, S. Sumatra. He made a beautiful water-colour drawing of Rafflesia arnoldi, present in the Buitenzorg Herbarium.

 

Collecting localities:

1918. S. Sumatra, Benkoelen (Jan.).-1931. E. Java: near Soember Telogo, Dampit; slope of G. Kawi; Ranoe Daroengan (S. slope G. Smeroe); private garden at Malang.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 20 nos from Java; Rafflesia material from Sumatra.
