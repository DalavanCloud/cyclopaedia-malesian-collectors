Brink, Rinse

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1902, Heer-Hugowaard, N.H., Holland.

 

career:

A zoologist who graduated (Ph. Dr) at Groningen University in 1925, and thereafter still was assistant at the Zoological Laboratory of the same university; teacher at the secondary school at Gorinchem, 1926-27; Group-Adviser employed at the Java Sugar Experiment Station (Pasoeroean), stationed at Solo, 1928-31; 2nd Inspector at the said Experiment station, at Pasoeroean, 1931-38; subsequently Representative ofthe combined Majang Estates and residing at Malang.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 2 nos of weeds from Solo (Centr. Java) (1931).
