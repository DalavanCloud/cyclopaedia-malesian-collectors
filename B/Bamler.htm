Bamler, Mrs

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Wife of missionary G. Bamler (see above).

 

Collecting localities:

In 1911 she collected ferns on the Sattelberg in former German New Guinea (Kaiser-Wilhelms-Land) intended for Prof. F. Foerster (Bretten). They were dealt with by Rosenstock (cf. sub Bamler, literature sub 5).

 

collections:

Herb. Rosenstock went to Berlin [B].
