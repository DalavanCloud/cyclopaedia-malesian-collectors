Barrett, Otis Warren

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1872, North Clarendon, Vt., U.S.A.

 

career:

An agriculturist who was Chief of the Division of Horticulture, Bur. of Agriculture, Manila, from 1910-14.

He is the author of’'Some new or little known Philippine economics’ (Philip. Agr. Rev. 6, 1913, p. 493-503, pl. 2-12).

 

collections:

He incidentally collected some plants for Herb. Manila [PNH].

 

biographical data:

Amer. Men of Sci. 1938.
