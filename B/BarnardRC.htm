Barnard, R.C.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Joined the Forest Department Malay Peninsula in August 1931. Nephew of the former. Retired as silviculturist from the Forest Research Station, Kepong, in 1956.

Author of many silvicultural papers.

 

Collecting localities:

Mainly collected in S. Perak.

 

collections:

Herb. Kuala Lump. [KEP], numbered in the C.F. (see sub Conservator of Forests) series.

 

biographical data:

Mal. For. Rec. 19, 1956, p. 122-123, with portr.
