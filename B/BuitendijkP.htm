Buitendijk, P.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Probably identical with the ship’s doctor of that name, who in the years 1904-14 brought home large zoological collections from his travels to the D.E. I.1

 

Collecting localities:

Plants from Sabang, P. Weh (N of Sumatra), collected in May 1914; and some from W. Java (Tandjong Priok).

 

collections:

Herb. Leiden [L]. His plants are referred to in papers by Lam and van Ooststroom; evidently they were not numbered.

 

literature:

(1) cf. A. Gijzen, ‘s Rijks Mus. v. Nat. Hist. 1820-1915, Rotterdam 1938, p. 152, 233 and 305.
