Boerrigter, Johan Herman

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1876, ’s Hertogenbosch, N.Br., Holland.

 

career:

Was employed by the D.E.I. Forest Service since 1898; stationed many years in the teak districts of Java (in 1901 at Gombong), at the end of 1916 at Soekaboemi and in 1921 at Buitenzorg. He was appointed Inspector in 1922, and retired in 1925.

 

Collecting localities:

1901. Java: Palorono; Gondanglegi, S of Malang (E. Java).1

 

collections:

Herb.Bog. [BO], the nos 64 and 82 S (the latter from Palorono) etc.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Bull. Jard. Bot. Buit. sér. 3, vol. 7, 1925, p. 350 and l.c. 12, 1932, p. 233.
