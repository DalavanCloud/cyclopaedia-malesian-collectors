Bradtke, Wilhelm

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: May 10, 1919.

 

career:

He is commemorated in Pseuderanthemum bradtkei Sp. le M. Moore.

 

Collecting localities:

Collected extensively in the Duke of York and Keravara Isls (Bism. Arch.) during World War I;1 and evidently some plants on the mainland of New Guinea too.2

 

collections:

In Herb. Brisbane [BRI]; Herb. Sydney [NSW]: phanerogams and ferns from New Guinea and New Ireland (pres. 1917). The numbering exceeds 349.

 

literature:

(1) Some of the collected species referred to by S. le M. Moore in ‘Acanthaceae Papuanae’ (Journ. Bot. 58, 1920, p. 190-195).

(2) Author of ‘The Flora of a small tropical island’' (Queensl. Naturalist 11, no 6) (non vidi).
