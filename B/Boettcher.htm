Boettcher

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Is cited as a collector of plants in the Philippines in Merrill, Enum. Philip. Fl. Pl., s.n., from Luzon, on Mt Pasoan etc.

A certain Georg Boettcher (Berlin 1890-1920), made entomological collections in the Philippines;1 he may be identical with the above-mentioned.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Entomol. Beih. Berl. Dahl. 1935, p. 23.
