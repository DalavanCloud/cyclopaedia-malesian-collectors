Boxall, Richard

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

(cf. also William Boxall, ? identical)

 

career:

Employed by the firm of Hugh Low and Co., to collect plants of horticultural value.

 

Collecting localities:

Malay Peninsula:1 Kedah Peak (with Sir Hugh Low) and the Settlements, about 1880.-Borneo.2-In 1891 Philippines3: in Samar and Mindanao.

 

collections:

Herb. Kew [K]: from Burma and Shan States (pres. 1890-91). In Herb. Vienna [W]: (? Philippine) orchids with Herb. Reichenbach.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5.

(2) Cited by Merrill, Enum. Born. Pl., 1921, p. 157, as the collector of Dendrobium bostrychodes Rchb.f.

(3) A certain Boxall (without initials) is cited by Merrill as a botanical collector in the Philippines in Bull. Bur. ofAgr. Manila no 4, 1903, p. 30.
