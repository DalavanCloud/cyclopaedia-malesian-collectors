Born: 1883, Edgewood, Iowa, U.S.A.

 

career:

Ethnologist in the Philippine Islands from 1905-14; Anthropologist in the University of Manila, Asst Prof. from 1914-23, from then onwards Professor.

He is commemorated in Boehmeria beyeri C.B.Rob.

 

collections:

He occasionally collected plants which were numbered in the Bur. of Science (B.S.) series (see there) and Philippine National Herbarium; in Herb. Manila [PNH], dupl. may be elsewhere.

 

biographical data:

Amer. Men of Sci. 1938.
