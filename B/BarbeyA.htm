Barbey, Auguste

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Addenda & Corrigenda)

 

Born: 1872, ? , Switzerland. Died: 1948 or ‘49.

 

career:

Son of William Barbey,1 who was educated at Munich and elsewhere in Natural Sciences (specially zoology and entomology) and subsequently in forestry; Dr Sci.; for years forester at Montcherand Sur Orbe (Vaud, Switzerland); retired several years ago; since four years paralyzed after a stroke.

He made a voyage round the world, evidently visiting Java in March 1891.

 

Collecting localities:

1891. Hongkong; W. Java (March): Buitenzorg Bot. Garden, Batavia (8), Sindanglaja (11), Bandoeng (12); Singapore (March); Ceylon, Calcutta.

 

collections:

Java plants, e.g. collected in the Botanic Gardens at Buitenzorg, in Herb. Deless. (Geneva [G]), and probably in Herb. Barbey-Boissier too. 158 nos of his ‘Tour du Monde’.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Biogr. notice in Bull. Soc. Bot. Genève 6, 1914, p. 220-240; Backer, Verkl. Woordenb., 1936.
