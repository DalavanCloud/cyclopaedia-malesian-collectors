Bartlett, Edward

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Succeeded G. Haviland as Curator of the Sarawak Museum at Kuching; he laid the foundation of the Zoological Department.

Author of several small zoological papers.1

 

collections:

Herb. Brit. Mus. [BM]: 338 nos from Sarawak, NW. Borneo (purch. 1899); dupl. in Herb. Leiden [L]; probably duplicates in Sarawak Herb. [SAR]. Ridley2 refers to a specimen dated May 17, 1893.

 

literature:

(1) In Sarawak Gazette 1894-96.

(2) In Kew Bull. 1938, p. 239.
