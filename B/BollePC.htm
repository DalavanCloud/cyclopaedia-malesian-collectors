Bolle, Pierrette Cornelie

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1893, Rotterdam, Holland. Died: 1945, in Japanese concentration camp at Semarang, Java.

 

career:

Phytopathologist who studied and took her Ph. Dr’s degree (1924) at Utrecht University; Assistant at the ‘Centraalbureau voor Schimmelcultures’, Baarn, Holland, 1924-25; employed at the Subdivision Cheribon of the Java Sugar Experiment Station, 1925-30; in the latter year transferred to the Agricultural Department of the said Station at Pasoeroean

She is the author of many phytopathological publications.

 

Collecting localities:

E. Java. 1927. Nongkodjadjar (G. Ketjiri), G. Tengger (Tosari, Moengal Pass).-1928. Pasoeroean.-1930. Soembermantjing.-1933. G. Ardjoeno; G.Tengger(with Posthumus).-Centr. Java. 1934. G. Merbaboe.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 36 nos + 1 no Bolle & Posthumus; Herb. Bog. [BO]: material of Rhodoleia teysmannii Miq. from G. Merbaboe (1934).
