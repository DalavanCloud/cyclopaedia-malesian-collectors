Brooke, Gilbert E.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Was a member of a small exploring party to look into the flora and fauna, and to do some clearing of the bush with a view to occupation at a later time in P. Jong, near Singapore. Small zoological and botanical collections were made during the trip.1 According to Mr Holttum he is not worth mentioning, he only sent few unsuitable ornamentals to Hort. Sing. (in 1924 and 1928).

 

literature:

(1) cf. Sing. Naturalist no 5, 1925, p. 47-50.
