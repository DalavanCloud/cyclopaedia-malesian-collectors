Bath, C.H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Is cited as the collector of Sindora supa Merr., nos 859 and 860 in Luzon (June 1904) (cf. Philip. Journ. Sci. vol. 1, Suppl., 1906, p. 199).

 

collections:

In 1903/04 3 nos of his from Tayabas, Luzon (F.B. series), were presented to Herb. Manila [PNH].
