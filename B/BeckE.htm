Beck, Dr Eugen

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Brother of the botanist Dr Gunther Beck, made a voyage to Aden, the East Indies, Further India, and Hongkong in c. 1886.1

 

collections:

Herb. Vienna [W]: about 300 plants from above-mentioned voyage. The collection includes some plants from P. Penang.2

 

literature:

(1) Cf. Jahresbericht über das Museum in Ann. Naturh. Mus. Wien 2,1887.

(2) Cf. Index Herb. pt II, 1954.
