Bosch, J.C.G. van den

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Several plants collected by him are cited in literature, e.g. Digitaria ciliaris Willd. from Weltevreden (W. Java),1 Panicum parvum Büse from the same locality,2 Ischaemum muticum L. from Tjilatjap (Centr. Java),3 Orthopogon compositus R.Br. from the latter locality,4 and other grasses; he also collected together with Junghuhn, and is mentioned in Miquel, Plantae Junghuhnianae.

 

collections:
