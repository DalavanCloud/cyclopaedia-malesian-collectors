Bogle, A. Lynn

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Ph.D. Minnesota; Field Research Assistant to Prof. Abbe (see there). In Asia he was accompanied by his wife (M.P. Bogle) at her own expense, except for the Kinabalu trip, for which NSF paid her expenses as well.

 

Collecting localities:

1961. India (Sept. 21-Oct. 6; Oct. 31-Dec. 12), Burma (Dec. 18-21).-1962. Malaya: Cameron Highlands (Jan. 13); Sabah: Mt. Kinabalu (Feb. 2-23) with Abbe, Kaul, and W. Meijer.

 

collections:

Dupl. in Herb. Edinb. [E]: main collection probably in Minnesota [MIN]. Primarily Fagaceae for Prof. Abbe were collected, but also material for a thesis.
