Bolhuis, Garbrand Gerhard

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1903, Veendam, Gr., Holland.

 

career:

Agriculturist, educated at the Agricultural College, Wageningen; since 1930 attached to the Subdivision for Annual Crops of the Agricultural Institute (Buitenzorg).

 

collections:

Sent Sesbania roxburghii Merr. from Borneo for identification to Herb. Bog. [BO]; at intervals Java plants for identification to Herb. Bog.[BO].
