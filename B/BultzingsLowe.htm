Bültzings Löwe

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Of Soerabaja.  This was probably G. von Bültzingslöwen, consular agent of the U.S.A., besides consul of the ‘Nord-Deutsche Bund’ (cf. portr. of G. von Bültzingslöwen, and monument for ... in L.S.A.M. von Römer, Historische schetsen, Batavia 1921, pl. 216-217).

 

collections:

Sent Cheilanthes farinosa from G. Ardjoena (E. Java) to Hort. Bog. in 1873.
