Connell, E.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Orchid grower1 at Alas Besoeki Estate, Loemadjang, collected many orchids in E. Java, principally at Loemadjang, Kraksaän and Prigi, about 1905-07.

 

collections:

Herb. [BO] and Hort. Bog.; many orchids described by J.J. Smith. He forwarded material to England too (cf. Bot. Mag. t. 8229).

 

literature:

(1) Author of: ‘Orchid growing and collecting in Java’ (Orch. Review 14, 1906, p. 257-261, 294-296).
