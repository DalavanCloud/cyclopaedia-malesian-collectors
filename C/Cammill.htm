Collecting localities

 

Cammill

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Collected Averrhoa bilimbi L., no 118, in Panay (Philippine Islands) in 1903 (cf. Pflanzenreich IV. 130, 1930, p. 419). The name may have been misread, and is probably Gammill (see there).
