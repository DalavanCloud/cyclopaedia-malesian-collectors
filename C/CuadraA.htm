Cuadra, Austin

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1925, Sandakan, Br. N. Borneo.

 

career:

Was appointed as Herbarium Assistant at Sandakan in 1947, in 1951 as Forest Guard. He resigned in 1951 and is now working with the Bakau & Kenya Extract Co. Ltd., Sandakan.

 

collections:

Cf. under Forest Department British North Borneo [SAN]. Collected in various parts of N. Borneo.
