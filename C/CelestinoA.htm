Celestino, A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Filipino, assistant to John Whitehead on a trip to Mindanao in 1895. He was with W. when the latter died in Hainan in 1899. Later he made ornithological collections for R.C. MacGregor (see there), and only occasionally collected some plants.

 

collections:

Few Philip. plants, numbered in the Bur. of Sci. series (see there); at Manila [PNH].
