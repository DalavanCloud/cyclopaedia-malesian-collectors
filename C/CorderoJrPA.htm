Cordero Jr, Paciente A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1941, Burauen, Leyte, Visayas, P.I.

 

career:

Was educated in botany at the Far Eastern University (B.S.); since December 1963 Botanical Collector and later Museum Technician in the Botany Division (Herbarium) of the National Museum; later in charge of the Algal section, a group in which he specializes. He is working on his Masters degree at Kagoshima University under Dr T. Tanaka.

 

Collecting localities:

1963. Manila and suburbs, Luzon; Lipuun I., Quezon, Palawan and nearby islets, with D.R. Mendoza and R.A. Espiritu.-1964. Palawan (Apr.-June). Batanes & Camiguin Islands together with a group of Kagoshima University, Japan (Nov. 9-30).-1965. Batan & Sobtan Islands (Apr. 9-May 7) with G.T. Velasquez and A. Timbol; Lubuagan, Kalinga-Apayao Prov., Luzon, with R.M. del Rosario.-1966. Puerto Galera, E. Mindoro, Luzon (with R.M. del Rosario and R.A. Espiritu); Bolinao, Pangasinan, Luzon.-1967. Luzon: Dalton Pass (with Mendoza) (Mar. 3-18); Biliran I. (May 2-June 3); Leyte; Visayas.

 

collections:

In the PNH [PNH] series, mostly Algae (> 1000).
