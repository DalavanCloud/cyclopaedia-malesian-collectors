Coelingh, Willemina Maria

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

First name changed to Willemina based on information by son, Derk Hille Ris Lambers.

 

Born: 1903, Amsterdam, Holland.

 

career:

A plant physiologist who got her education and took her degree (1929) at Utrecht University; Assistant Physiologist at the Java Sugar Experiment Station, Pasoeroean, 1929-32; subsequently she married Dr M. Hille Ris Lambers, Selectionist in the employ of the Malang Experiment Station (E. Java).

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS, now in BO]: 26 nos from the N. slope of G. Kawi, E. Java, in 1932.
