In 1947/48 he collected on Mt Isarog in Luzon, 189 nos in the P.N.H. series.

Of the staff of the Philippine National Museum, occasionally collected plants in Luzon, and with Mendoza (see there) in Mindanao (March 1949); > 200 nos, numbered in the P.N.H.series (cf. sub Philippine National Herbarium).
