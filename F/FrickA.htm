Collections

 

Frick, Dr A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

collections:

Mus. Techn. Coll. Zürich [ZT?]: natural history objects collected in the Buitenzorg Botanical Garden in 1889. It is dubious whether these ought to be regarded as collected by Frick, as a case containing the specimens was dispatched by the Botanic Garden to Dr Frick o/b s.s. Soenda, Tandjong Priok harbour, for Prof. Schroeter.
