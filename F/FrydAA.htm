Fryd, Aage August (not with ij)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1898, Kalundborg, Denmark.

 

career:

‘Boscharchitect’ in the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service; since 1921 stationed in the teak districts of Java; in Dec. 1928 transferred to W. Borneo, Pontianak; in June 1931 to Sumatra West Coast, Fort de Kock; since 1935 in the teak districts in Java again. In 1949-50 finally ‘bosbeheerder’ of Pemalang, stationed at Tegal. In the latter year he left Java and returned to Denmark.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO] and Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: from W. Borneo (1929-31), numbered in the F.R.I. series (see sub Forest Research Institute).
