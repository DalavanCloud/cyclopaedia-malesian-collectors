Fryar, Donald

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1909, Gympie, Queensland, Australia. Died: 1954.

 

career:

Forest Ranger who collected plants in the Morobe District, NE. New Guinea. In 1953 stationed at Rabaul, New Britain.

 

collections:

Dupl. in Herb. Leiden [L]; high numbers in the N.G.F. series (cf. New Guinea Forces) [LAE].
