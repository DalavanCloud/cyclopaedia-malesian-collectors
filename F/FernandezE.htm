Fernandez, Emanuel

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A collector employed by W. Griffith (see there) in Malacca while he was at Calcutta and presumably during his first residence in Malacca (1841-42) (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem 4, 1927, nos 4-5).

 

collections:

In Herb. Griffith (see there; in Calcutta [CAL], Kew [K], etc.).
