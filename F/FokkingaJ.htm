Fokkinga, Johannes

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1895, Augustinusga, Fr., Holland.

 

career:

Forest Officer, since 1921 in the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service; stationed in Java, in teak districts, and elsewhere. In 1947 he was appointed Chief Inspector of the Forest Service.

 

Collecting localities:

Centr. Java: Pekalongan Res. (June and Oct. 1923).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: plants collected in teak forests on behalf of Beumée (see there).
