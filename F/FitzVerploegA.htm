Fitz Verploeg, A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Resident of Bagelen, Centr. Java, who started his career as a clerk to the Director of Agriculture and then entered the Civil Service (e.g. Asst Resident of Palembang). In later years Director of the Civil Service and finally Resident of Pasoeroean in E. Java.

 

collections:

He forwarded Java plants to Hort. Bog. in 1869; possibly no dried material preserved.

 

biographical data:

Ind. Gids 10 1, 1888, p. 266.
