(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Died: 1945, during Japanese occupation of Malaya.

 

career:

A Danish planter, arrived in Malaya in 1912 in the employ of the Danish East Asiatic Company; until 1928 planter on Mount Austin Rubber Estates, subsequently transferred to Teluk Merbau Coconut and Rubber Plantations, Sepang, Selangor, as General Manager. He is interested in orchids, Nepenthes and other plants.

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]: orchids (pres. 1939). According to Mr Holttum he collected in several parts of Malaya, and forwarded some specimens to the herbarium, from 1939 onwards.
