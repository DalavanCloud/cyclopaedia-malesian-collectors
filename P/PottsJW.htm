Biographical data

 

Potts, J.W.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

1885. SE. New Guinea, Papua. Thursday Isl. and Prince of Wales Isl. (Sept.), and return to Thursday Isl. Tour by boat in the schooner ‘Elsea’ leaving Thursday Isl. on Oct. 1; some islands in Torres Straits; Port Moresby (Oct. 11-12, trip to Koitapu); a trip to the sources of Laloki River, and subsequently sailing for the Cloudy and Baxter Bay via Hula, Kerepuna, Kalo (= Kemp Welch) River, ascending the same for 20 miles;1 back at Port Moresby; sailing for Bola, Lilley Isl., Delena (Hall Sound), Maiva, Moti-Motu; Thursday Isl. (after absence of 6 weeks).

 

collections:

In 1886 he returned to Sydney with a collection of weapons and other ethnological objects, 60 species of birds of paradise and numerous orchids. At least partly in the Queensland Museum.

 

literature:

(1) J.W. Potts: ‘Notes on a trip to New Guinea’ (Proc. Roy. Soc. Queens). 3, Brisb. 1887, p. 2-10); cf. also Wichmann, Entd. Gesch. N.G., in Nova Guinea 22, p. 393.
