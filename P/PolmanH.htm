Polman, Harm

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1890, De Wijk, Dr., Holland.

 

career:

For more than 12 years attached to the Government Caoutchouc Industry (D.E.I.) and in private horticulture; since 1927 Horticultural Officer in Government employ, stationed successively at Tosari (Tengger Distr.) (1927-30), and at Malang (1930-41), both in E. Java.

 

Collecting localities:

1928. E. Java: G. Tengger, at Tosari etc. (Aug.-Sept.).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 11 nos of Loranthaceae on behalf of the monographer B.H. Danser.
