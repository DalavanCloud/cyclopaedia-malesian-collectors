InformatioN:

About 1935 Dr Quisumbing had the name of the herbarium at Manila changed to ‘Philippine National Herbarium’, and commenced a new series of collections from various sources made by members of his staff to replace the older ‘Bureau of Science’ (see there) series. As the records were destroyed during the war, a new series starting with ‘Phil. Nat. Herb. 1’ was begun in 1947. Though the material at Manila has been destroyed, duplicates of the former P.N.H. series have been distributed, and there will be duplications, but Mr Quisumbing is sure there will be no duplication of names.

collecting localities:

Several of the 1955 collections were made in Jan.-Feb. on Mt Maquiling, Laguna Prov., Luzon, possibly by participants of a forestry course.

 

collections:

Herb. Manila [PNH]; dupl. to Leiden [L], Kew [K], Arn. Arbor. [A], Sing. [SING], U.S. Nat. Mus. [US], Bogor [BO], etc.
