Pritzel, Ernst

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Died: at the end of the war, c. 1945.

 

career:

Ph.D., botanist (taxonomy and plant geography), about 1900 teacher in Berlin. He accompanied F.L.E. Diels (see there) on some of his travels.

 

collections:

Australia plants (pres. 1901) in Herb. State Mus. Stockholm [S]: Possibly including some from Malaysia. ? Herb. Berlin [B].
