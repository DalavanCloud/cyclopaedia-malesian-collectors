Prillwitz, Peter Maximilian Hermann Hubertus

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1900, Garoet, Java.

 

career:

Educated at the Agricultural College, Wageningen; till 1936 on the staff of the General Tea Experiment Station (later W. Java Exp. Stat.); since 1936 high official in the Department for Economic Affairs at Batavia.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: material of Balanophora elongata, collected on G. Slamat, Centr. Java, in Sept. 1928.
