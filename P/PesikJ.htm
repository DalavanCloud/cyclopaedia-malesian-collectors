Pesik, J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Author of some typed reports on Central Celebes.1

 

Collecting localities:

Centr. Celebes: Donggala and Paloe (Nov. 1928, 1930-31); Kolonedale (1933).

 

collections:

Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: bb. nos from Paloe and Donggala (1930-31), and from Kolonedale (1933). Herb. Bog. [BO]: 25 nos of herbaceous plants (pres. through the intermediary of the F.R.I.) and dupl. of the F.R.I.

 

literature:

(1) On Tawaéli Distr. (1928), Paloe (1930) and Donggala (1931); in the archives of the F.R.I. at Buitenzorg.
