Parks, John Damper

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

fl. 1823-1838.

 

career:

(Sometimes misspelled Parkes). Collected for the Horticultural Society in China and Java, 1823.1 Afterwards gardener of the Earl of Arran, F.H.S. at Bognor, Sussex.

 

Collecting localities:

Left London in the ‘Lowther Castle’ in 1823, returning in the same ship in May 1824. On the outward voyage he landed at Anger Point (= Anjer, W. Java) (Aug. 1-3. 1823), gathering some botanical specimens; on the homebound voyage the anchor was cast at North Island in the Straits of Sunda, to take in water (Jan. 24, 1824).

 

collections:

Sold by the Horticultural Society in 1856, obtained by Bentham; in Herb. Kew [K].2 A Journal kept by him from August 1823 to 1824 is in the possession of the Roy. Hort. Society.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Transact. Hortic. Soc. 5, 1824, p. 427; MS. Journal in the Roy. Hort. Soc.

(2) cf. Journ. Bot. 32, 1894, p. 298-299.

‘A Catalogue of all the valuable collections of dried plants which have been formed by the travellers employed by the Hort. Soc. of Lond. etc. (1856).

 

biographical data:

Biogr. Index Britten & Boulger in Journ. Bot. 28, 1890, p. 117, and in 2nd ed. by Rendle, 1931; Bretschneider, Hist. Bet. Discov. China .1898, p. 271-274; J. R. Hort. Soc. 80, 1955, p. 270.
