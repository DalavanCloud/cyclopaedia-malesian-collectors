Doorn, Zeger van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1891, Zegveld, U., Holland.

 

career:

Forest Officer, educated at the Agricultural College at Wageningen, who went to the D.E.I. in 1915; finally Chief of Forest Management. He retired in 1946, and returned to Holland.

Author of some sylvicultural papers.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: Arthrocnemum indictum Moq. from the S. coast of P. Kangean, NE of Java (coll. March 1940).

 

biographical data:

Tectona 38, 1948, p. 1-2.
