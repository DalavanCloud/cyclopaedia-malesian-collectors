Dranen, van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

When employed at the People’s Loan-bank at Fort de Kock (Sumatra West Coast), collected a living orchid which was cultivated in Hort. Jacobson (see there) at Fort de Kock. The plant came from Agam in the same residency. Material was sent to Buitenzorg.
