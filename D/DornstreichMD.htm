Dornstreich, M.D.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Collecting localities:

Made an ethnobotanical collection in the Territory of New Guinea, >246 nos, numbered in the NGF-M.D.D. series. East Sepik region was visited in Sept. 1968.

 

collections:

In Herb. Lae [LAE]; some dupl. in Leiden Herb. [L].
