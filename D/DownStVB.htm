Down, St Vincent B.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Merchant of Singapore and afterwards of Sarawak; for many years on the Gardens Committee (-1919), Singapore (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).

 

Collecting localities:

Malay Peninsula. In 1900 a few plants at the mouth of the Telubin River, Patani, Siam.-In 1911 on the Bekok and Sedili rivers in Johore.

 

collections:

Herb. and Hort. Sing. [SING] (pres. in 1916, 1919, etc).
