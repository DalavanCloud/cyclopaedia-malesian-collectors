Diest, Pieter Hendrik van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1835, Edam, N.H., Holland.

 

career:

Mining engineer, educated at the Technical College, Delft; joined the D.E. Indian Government service in 1857; in 1870 on sick-leave to Holland, and retired in 1873.

 

Collecting localities:

He is cited by Miquel (in Ann. Mus. Bot. Lugd. Bat. 1, 1863-64) as the collector of Hepaticae on G. Maras in Banka. He travelled across the island for seven years (1858-65).1

 

collections:

Material in Herb. Leiden [L].

 

literature:

(1) Author of ‘Bangka beschreven in reistogten’ (Amsterdam 1865 + 2 maps), and of some other papers.
