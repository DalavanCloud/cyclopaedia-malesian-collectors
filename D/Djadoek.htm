Djadoek

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Native collector from Buitenzorg, who was in the employ of the Deli Experiment Station, Medan (Sumatra), in 1915. With Roesil (see there) he was to make a collection of the vegetation on fallow tobacco lands.

 

Collecting localities:

Sumatra East Coast. 1915. G. Singgalang (Dec. 4), G. Pinto (Dec. 12).

 

collections:

Herb. Deli Exp. Stat., Medan, identified at Buitenzorg. From G. Pinto e.g. no 983; numbered in a series.
