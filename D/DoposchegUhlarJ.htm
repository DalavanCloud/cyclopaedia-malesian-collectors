Doposcheg-Uhlár, Dr Joseph

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

About 1913 Assistant at the Botanical Laboratory of Munich University. He stayed at Buitenzorg (Java) from Apr.-July 1913, making studies on cleistogamy, anisophylly, etc. In 1931 Major, living at Partenkirchen, Bavaria.

 

collections:

Plants and materials were collected for the Bot. Garden and the Lab. for Plant Physiology of the Univ. of Munich.1

 

literature:

(1) cf. Dammerman in Ann. Jard. Bot. Buit. 45, 1935, p. 39.
