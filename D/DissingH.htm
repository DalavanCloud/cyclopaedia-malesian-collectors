Dissing, Henry

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1931, Hansted, Denmark.

 

career:

Was educated at the University of Copenhagen (B.Sc.), later Assistant at the Mycological Institute. He specializes in Fungi (genus Helvella).1

 

Collecting localities:

From July 16-Sept. 5, 1962, he participated in the Danish ‘Noona Dan Expedition’ (see there for Itinerary, Collections, etc.).

 

literature:

(1) Cf. Dansk Bot. Ark. 25 (1966) p. 1-172.
