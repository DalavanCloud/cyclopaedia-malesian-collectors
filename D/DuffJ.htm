Duff, John

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Till 1884 official of the Botanic Garden at Sydney, who collected Nephrolepis duffii Th. Moore (cf. Backer, Verkl. Woordenb., 1936) in Duke of York Island (Bismarck Archipelago).1 As the plant was described in 1878, the collecting must have taken place previously.

 

collections:

Plants and seeds on behalf of the Bot. Gard. Sydney.

 

literature:

(1) cf. also Hortus Veitchii 1906, p. 325.
