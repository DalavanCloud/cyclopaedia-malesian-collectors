Dore, Dr John

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

collections:

Of the Federation of Malaya’s Agricultural Department occasionally presents plants to the Sing. Herb. [SING]. They have all been collected by officers of the Department of Agriculture in the course of their normal duties, and then forwarded to the Federal Headquarters at Kuala Lumpur for identification; interesting specimens are forwarded to the Botanic Gardens in Singapore. Cf. also sub Department of Agriculture.
