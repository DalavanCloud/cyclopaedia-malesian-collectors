Diakonoff, Alexey

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1907, St Petersburg, Russia.

 

career:

Entomologist, educated at Amsterdam University; on the staff of the Experiment Station for the Java Sugar Industry at Pasoeroean; since end 1941 Entomologist of the Zoological Museum at Buitenzorg.

 

Collecting localities:

E. Java: Trètès on the N. slope of G. Ardjoeno (March 4, 1940); on the S. slope of G. Smeroe.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: some foster-plants of insects.
