Dammerboer, J.G.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1867, Oeta Rimbaroe, Sumatra.

 

career:

Education expert, collected some orchids in Java (1918), which he forwarded to J.J. Smith.

Dendrobium dammerboerii J.J.Sm. was named after him (cf. Backer, Verkl. Woordenb., 1936).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: some orchids.
