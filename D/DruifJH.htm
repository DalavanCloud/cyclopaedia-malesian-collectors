Druif, Dr Jan Henrie

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1893, Rotterdam, Holland

 

career:

Geologist, educated at the Wageningen Agricultural College, the Technical College at Delft, at Utrecht University, and at the Hanover Technical College (Germany). On the staff of the Deli Experiment Station (Sumatra); in 1939 appointed Pedologist at the Soil Science Institute at Buitenzorg; in 1946 transferred to the Makassar (SW. Celebes) branch of the General Experiment Station.

 

Collecting localities:

1939. S. Sumatra (betw. Apr. 8-May 11): Tebingtinggi (Palembang), Lahat, Pagaralam and G. Dempo; during an exploration trip by car along the main roads of Palembang Residency.

 

collections:

Hort. Bog.: living orchids.
