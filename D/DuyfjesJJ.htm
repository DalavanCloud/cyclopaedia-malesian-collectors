Duyfjes, Jacobus Johan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1876, Haarlem, Holland. Died: 1938, The Hague, Holland.

 

career:

Forest Officer, since 1897 in the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service; in the beginning stationed in various regions of Central Java, in 1910 in charge of the Forest Administration of E. Priangan; in 1914 in the Head Office (Buitenzorg), in 1917 transferred to Bandoeng; in 1920 appointed Inspector and in 1925 Head Inspector; retired in 1926.

 

Collecting localities:

W. Java, Priangan Res.: Limbangan Distr. near Pangentjongan; G. Patoeha (July 1910, March 1914).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: orchids for J.J. Smith.
