Docters van Leeuwen-Reynvaan, Jenny

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1880, Amsterdam, Holland. Died: 1963, Amersfoort, U., Netherlands.

 

career:

Biologist, educated at Amsterdam University; teacher at a secondary school at Gouda, 1906-07; in 1907 she married W.M. Docters van Leeuwen (see there) whom she followed to Java.

With her husband she published several papers on galls.

 

Collecting localities:

E. Java: environs of Nongkodjadjar (Kletak) (Oct. 1920-Febr. 1921).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: Nongkodjadjar plants. Galls in private Herb. Docters van Leeuwen, not numbered.
