Djaja Atmadja

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1895.

 

career:

Assistant Teacher of Agriculture since 1919; stationed successively on the Govt Gutta-percha Estate Tjipetir (near Tjibadak in W. Java), in E. Priangan (1920), and at Tjipanas (1924); Assistant Consulting Agriculturist since 1925, stationed at Tjipanas (till 1931); 1931-32 employed at the Normal School at Poerwakarta; in the year 1932 for some months stationed at Tabanan, Lombok (Lesser Sunda Isls). He was dismissed at his own request; before the war leader of a course on retail trade for the Dept of Education and Public Worship.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 5 nos collected near Tjioedjoeng, Bantam Res., W. Java.
