Career

 

Doubrowine, Dr N.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

From Moscow, stayed during two months, between May and Sept. 1885, at the Foreigner’s Laboratory at Buitenzorg, Java. In the first place he wished to make a general survey of the Netherlands Indian flora by means of the Buitenzorg Garden before making an expedition through the Archipelago. In the second place he intended to bring together collections for the Botanical Museum Moscow. (cf. Dammerman in Ann. Jard. Bot. Buit. 45, 1935, p. 11-12).

He visited at least Billiton (cf. Versl. Pl. Tuin for 1885, p. 12).
