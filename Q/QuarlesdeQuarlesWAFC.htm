Quarles de Quarles, Baron W.A.F.C.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

A resident of Toeloengagoeng in Kediri Residency (E. Java), collected orchids at Trenggalek in the same residency and sent them to J.J. Smith, Herb. Bog. [BO] (pres. before 1910).
