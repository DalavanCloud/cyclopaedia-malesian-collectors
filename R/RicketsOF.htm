Rickets, O.F.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: c. 1867, England.

 

career:

Civil servant in Sarawak, NW. Borneo; for many years Resident at Kuching, in 1938 still living there. He was very interested in plants, and collected many species for his garden in Borneo.1 As early as 1882 he climbed Mt Penrissen!

 

collections:

Hort. Sing.: living plants from Sarawak (pres. 1918, 1938). According to Mr Holltum his name appears on a few specimens in Herb. Sing. [SING] taken from living plants; he made no dried collections himself.

 

literature:

(1) Author of ‘Orchids of Sarawak’ (Orch. Review 1936, p. 77-83).
