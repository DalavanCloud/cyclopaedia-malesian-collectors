Rothdauscher, Heinrich

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Pharmacist. Took his Ph.Dr’s degree at Munich in 1897. He is cited by Merrill1 as the collector of botanical material in the Philippines.

 

collections:

Elsewhere we came across the statement that he is the collector of Sciaphila nutans Giessen in Luzon, P.I. (1879), the type specimen of which in Herb. Munich [M],2 and of Averrhoa carambola, Manila (Luzon) (1879); another plant was collected at Manila in 1883 and is preserved in Herb. Munich [M].3

In Herb. Munich [M]: 338 nos from the Philippines (pres. 1879).

 

literature:

(1) In Bull. Bur. of Agric. Manila no 4; cited as Rothdaucher!

(2) cf. Pflanzenreich 104, 1938, p. 48.

(3) cf. l.c. 98 a, 1932, p. 602.
