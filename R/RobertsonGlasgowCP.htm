Robertson-Glasgow, Charles Ponsonby

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: about 1900.

 

career:

Visited Singapore and Perak in 1898.

 

Collecting localities:

1898. Malay Peninsula: Singapore and Perak (Gapis Pass).

 

collections:

Herb. Kew [K]: chiefly Fungi; the greater part of his collection seems to have been lost upon his death (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).
