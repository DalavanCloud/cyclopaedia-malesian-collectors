Career

 

Rojo, Justo P.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Of the Forestry Department, School of Agriculture, Los Baños, Luzon. From Nov. 1968 he did post-graduate work on plant taxonomy at the Rijksherbarium, Leiden, with a one-year grant Now Chief of the Forests Products Collectior Section under the Wood Technology Service, National Science Development Board, College, Laguna.

He collected with William L. Stern (see there).
