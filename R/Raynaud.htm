Raynaud

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A certain François Raynaud figures on the list of the crew in the History of the Voyage of the ‘Astrolabe’ (cf. sub P.A. Lesson) as ‘maitre canonnier’, but it is more likely that the actual collector is A.A.M. Reynaud (see there).

 

collections:

Is cited by Koster (in Blumea 1, 1935, p. 432) as the collector of Vernonia patula (Dryand.) Merr. var. typica, s.n., in W. Java; the cited specimen in Herb. Leiden [L].
