Rowell, Dr Irvine

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: 1892, ? London, England.

 

career:

From about 1878-90 Head of the Medical Service in the Straits Settlements; a friend of G. King.

In 1888 he sent in a collection of wood samples to an exhibition in England. He might have collected some plants.

Ficus rowelliana King was named after him (cf. Backer, Verkl. Woordenb., 1936).
