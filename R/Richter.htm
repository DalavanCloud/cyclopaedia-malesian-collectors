Richter

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Possibly Benjamin Richter, surgeon-major of the lifeguards of the Dutch Governor-General, who had his will drawn up in Jan. 27, 177 (Ara, VOC 6851, nr. 1581). One of the executioners of this will was Christ. Kleynhoff (see there).

 

collections:

Is cited as the collector of Vitex bicolor Willd. in Java in 1773 (cf. Hallier in Meded. ‘s Rijks Herb. no 37, 1918, p. 42).
