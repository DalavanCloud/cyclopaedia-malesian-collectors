Rudolph, Karl

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

German Government Gardener, from 1907-10 stationed in Neu Pommern (= New Britain).

 

collections:

Herb. Berl. [B]: 40 nos from the Gazelle Peninsula near Simpsonhafen (= Rabaul), New Britain; dried fruits and wood samples in the Show Mus. Berl.

Plants were published in ‘Beitrige zur Flora Papuasiens’ (Engl. Bot. Jahrb. 1912→).
