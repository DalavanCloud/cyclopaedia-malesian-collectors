Roy van Zuydewyn, E. de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Living at Poeger, SE. Java.

 

Collecting localities:

In 1930 he collected material of Andropogon contortus near Poeger, S of Menampoe (S. coast), at the instigation of Th. Etty (see there).

 

collections:

Mr Etty forwarded the material to Herb. Bog. [BO].
