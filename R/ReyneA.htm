Reyne, Adriaan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1890, Uitgeest, N.H., Holland.

 

career:

Entomologist; on the staff of the Agricultural Experiment Station in Surinam till 1925; then teacher at a secondary school at Haarlem and taking his Ph. Dr’s degree at Utrecht in 1926; in 1927 appointed in the employ of the Institute for Plant Diseases at Buitenzorg, and from 1929-33 Head of the Coconut Experiment Station at Manado (N. Celebes). After expiry of his European leave he was put on half-pay and stayed in Holland, where he was pensioned off in 1938.

He is the author of some papers on, and in relation with, the coconutpalm.

 

Collecting localities:

1932. N. Celebes: Lolak Estate (March 8).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: a few plants.
