Rijn van Alkemade, Ingebrecht Abraham van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1858, Rotterdam, Holland. Died: 1932, The Hague, Holland

 

career:

Joined the D.E. Indian Civil Service in 1878; in 1900 promoted Resident of Palembang, S. Sumatra.

He is commemorated in Pandanus alkemadei Martelli.

 

Collecting localities:

He sent material of above-mentioned plant from Ogan-Oeloe Division, Palembang Res., S. Sumatra, to Martelli (cf. Webbia 4, 1913, p. 430-431).

 

collections:

Material in Herb. Martelli (= Florence [FI]).
