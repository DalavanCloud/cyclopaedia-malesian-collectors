Renesse van Duivenbode, van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

He was probably a descendant of M.D. van Renesse van Duivenbode whom A.R. Wallace (in Malay Arch. 2, p. 2) called the King of Ternate.

 

collections:

Sent living material of Nepenthes from Halmaheira (Moluccas) to Hort. Bog., in 1908.
