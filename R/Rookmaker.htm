Rookmaker

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Was probably identical with the civil officer H.R. Rookmakers, who made a small ornithological collection in Bone, SW. Celebes, in 1912.1

 

collections:

Sent a living orchid from Bone near Pampano(e)a (SW. Celebes) to Hort. Bog. The plant is cited in a publication of J.J. Smith.

 

literature:

(1) Cf. G.C.A. Junge in Ardea 41, Jub. no, 1954, p. 327.
