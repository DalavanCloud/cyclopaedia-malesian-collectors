Rosenbluth, R.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Forest Officer of the Bureau of Forestry, Manila, P.I.

Diospyros rosenbluthii Elmer was named after him.

 

collections:

Herb. Manila [PNH], numbered in the F.B. (see sub Forestry Bureau) series, incl. plants from Mindoro (May 1908) and Masbate (Apr. 1909). Also collected together with Tamesis. Small collections. 65 Dupl. in U.S. Nat. Herb. Wash. [US].
