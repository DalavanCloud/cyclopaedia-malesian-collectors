Rosenstingl, Walther

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Physician at Gmunden (Austria), lived for some years in Sumatra West Coast.

 

Collecting localities:

He collected at Soeliki, Padang Highlands,1 at L Kota, Mangani (Dec. 22, 1922), etc.

 

collections:

In 1928 he sent 2 Sumatra plants for identification to Leiden Herbarium [L]. He then lived at Enns, Austria.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Oesterr. Bot. Zeitschr. 79, 1930, p. 352-354.
