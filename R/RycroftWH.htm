Rycroft, Sir William Henry

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1861, Kempshott Park, Basingstoke, England. Died: 1925, Perth, Scotland.

 

career:

Educated at Eton and Sandhurst, with a military career mainly in Africa; on retired pay in 1921. Major-General, Governor of British North Borneo from 1922-25.

 

Collecting localities:

Made collections of plants at Sandakan, Br. N. Borneo, on B(a)lambangan Isl.,1 etc., about 1923.

 

collections:

Herb. Brit. Mus. [BM]. His plants have printed labels.

 

literature:

(1) Cf. Jonker, Monograph ofthe Burmanniaceae, 1938, p. 125.

 

biographical data:

Who was who, 1916-1928.
