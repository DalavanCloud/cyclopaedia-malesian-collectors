Ramali, A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Physician of the Service of Public Health etc. in the D.E.I., stationed at Tandjong Redep (NE. Borneo).

 

collections:

Sent medicinal plants from there for identification to Herb. Bog. [BO] in 1941. The material was in a poor state.
