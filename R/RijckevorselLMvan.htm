Rijckevorsel, L.M. van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1884, Vught, N.Br., Holland. Died: 194. , in Japanese prisoner camp, Java.

 

career:

Father of the Society of Jesus, stationed in Central Java, viz at Moentilan from 1909-14 and 1919-27 (during World War I in Holland), at Ambarawa 1927-36, at Moentilan again 1936-38, and at Mertojoedan 1938-39.

 

Collecting localities:

1913. Centr. Java: Kedoe Residency, also on G. Andong near Djokja. -1922. W. Java (Apr.): G. Papandajan, lava streams G. Goentoer, G. Kendang and Kawah Manoek.-1930. Centr. Java (Jan.): neighbourhood of Ambarawa (collecting Cuscuta australis).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: > 153 nos.
