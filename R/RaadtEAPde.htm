Raadt, Eugène Alphonse Pieter de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1911, Malang, Java.

 

career:

Finished his education in 1934 at Leiden University and subsequently joined the D.E. Indian Civil Service. In 1947 District Officer at Palembang, S. Sumatra.

 

Collecting localities:

1947. S. Sumatra: Palembang and vicinity, and near Talang Betoetoe (Aug.).-1948. Poelau Panggoeng (Oct. 15), Palembang (Nov. 3).-S. Sumatra: Palembang, at least till May 1950.

 

collections:

Herb. Leiden [L]: 88 nos.
