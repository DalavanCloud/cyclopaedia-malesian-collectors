Rayner, Laurence Tibor

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1911, Budapest, Hungrary.

 

career:

Architect who attended the Technical College in Budapest from 1929-34, and emigrated to Australia in 1939. Till 1950 he was employed in Sydney, from 1950-56 Senior Designing Architect for the Victorian Government in Melbourne, and at present in private practice there. He has a special interest in mountaineering, nature study, and photography.

 

Collecting localities:

Together with his friend N.M. Semple (see there) he collected plants in New Guinea in 1953.
