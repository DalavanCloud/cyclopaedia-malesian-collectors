Roos, R.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: Jan. 3, 1919, near kp. Sempoeang, Atjeh, Sumatra.

 

career:

Civil Administrator at Serbodjadi, Atjeh (N. Sumatra), successor of Baptist (see there).

 

collections:

Sent material of Rafflesia sp. in spirit to Koorders;1 in Herb. Koorders = Herb. Bog. [BO].

 

literature:

(1) cf. S.H. Koorders: ‘Botanisch overzicht der Rafflesiaceae van Ned. Indië’ (Meded. N.I. Ver. t. Nat. Besch. no 4, 1918, p. 110-111).
