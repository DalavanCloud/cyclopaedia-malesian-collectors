Radermacher, Arnold

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1886, Aix-la-Chapelle, Germany.

 

career:

Botanist, passed the government examination at Munich and was for some years a teacher in Germany. He continued his botanical studies in Holland and took his Ph.Dr’s degree in 1925 at Utrecht University; in the same year appointed Group-Adviser in the employ of the Experiment Station for the Java Sugar Industry at Pasoeroean and successively stationed at Modjokerto (1926-32) and Pasoeroean (1932-36). In 1937 Director of the boardingschool Simpang College at Lawang; subsequently living at Vienna and in Yugo-Slavia, in the latter country attached to the Botanical Museum at Split; in 1942 he was at the ‘Hygienski Zavod’, Spalato, Dalmatia.

 

Collecting localities:

E. Java. In 1927: G. Ardjoeno; in Soerabaja Res., NE of G. Bolang, at Modjokerto and near sugar factories; W. Java: G. Patoeha (1927); E. Java: Soemberdjati (1928); G. Ardjoeno (1929); G. Andjasmoro (1930): Kawah Idjen and G. Raoeng (1933); Batoe Oeloe in Besoeki (1934).

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: type specimen of Blumea pachycephala Kosterm. in Herb. Leiden [L] (cf. Blumea 4, 1941, p. 488).
