Collections

 

Middendorp, W.A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Amsterdam, Holland, presented a collection of herbarium from the Karo Lands (Sumatra East Coast) to the Herb. Kol. (= Ind.) Inst. Amsterdam in 1920.

This was a duplicate set of a collection made in 1919 by the native collector Galoengi (see there) on behalf of Lörzing. At the time Middendorp was District Officer there.
