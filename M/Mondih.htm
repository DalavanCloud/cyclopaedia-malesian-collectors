Mondih

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

‘Mantri’ in the employ of the Buitenzorg Herbarium; in 1931 temporarily placed at the disposal of the Forest Officer in W. Borneo, J.P. Schuitemaker (see there).

 

Collecting localities:

1931. W. Borneo (March 11-16): neighbourhood of Pontianak, near Sg. Raja and Sg. Poetat; Karimata Islands: P. Penebangan (March 19-20), P. Tiangbalai (21-22), P. Karimata (23), P. Seroetoe (24-25), P. Karimata besar (25-26) and from March 27-28 visiting P. Pelobang, Boeroeng and Penebangan; returning to W. Borneo: Pontianak (March 31), collecting near kp. Andjongan (Apr. 5-6, visiting G. Bala) and near kp. Mandor (Oetan Kopiang and Oetan Laosengkong) (Apr. 7-8).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 234 nos from Borneo and 151 from Karimata Islands. Duplicates in Herb. Leiden [L] and Utrecht [U].
