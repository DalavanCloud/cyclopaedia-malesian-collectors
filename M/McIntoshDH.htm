McIntosh, Donald Henry

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1927, Sydney, N.S.W., Australia.

 

career:

Graduate from the Australian Forestry School and New South Wales University; Forest Officer, Keravat, New Britain, after 1945.

Author of  ‘The effect of man on the forests of the Highlands of Eastern New Guinea’ (Symp. Humid Trop. Veget. Unesco, Goroka 1960, 1963, p. 123-126).

 

Collecting localities:

Small collections in New Britain.

 

collections:

Numbered in the N.G.F. series (cf. New Guinea Forces) [LAE].
