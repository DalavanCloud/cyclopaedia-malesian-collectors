Morley, R.J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Research Assistant at the Geography Department, University of Hull, England, accompanied J.R. Flenley on a tour to W. Java and Sumatra in 1972.

 

Collecting localities:

1972. Sumatra and W. Java (see under Flenley). After Flenley’s departure he continued to Malaysia, and visited Tasek Bera in Malaya, and Mt Kinabalu in Sabah (N. Borneo). He returned to Britain on Nov. 4.

 

collections:

With Flenley (see there) and M.K. Kardin, and alone nos 1-48 (Sabah), and 49-98 (Malaya). In Herb. Hull Univ. [HLU?], etc.
