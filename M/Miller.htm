Miller

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is cited as the collector of material of a Sapindacea from Malacca, s.n., in Herb. Calcutta [CAL] (cf. Radlkofer in Act. d. Congr. Internat. d. Bot. etc. a Amsterdam 1877, 1878, p. 39), and of a Myristicacea from Malacca, in Herb. Leiden [L] and Munich [M] (cf. Warburg, Monogr. Myristic., 1897, p. 604); cf. also sub C. Miller.
