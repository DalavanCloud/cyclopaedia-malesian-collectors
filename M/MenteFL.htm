Mente, Frans Lodewijk

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1894, Gajan (Blora), Rembang, Java.

 

career:

‘Boscharchitect’, since 1912 in the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service; during his term of office stationed in Central and East Java.

 

Collecting localities:

E. Java, Besoeki Res.: between the rivers Tanggoel and Petoeng (June 1920); environs of Djember (1924).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: plants collected in 1920 (probably the nos 1676-1696), numbered in the series of Beumée (see there); dupl. of the F.R.I. (coll. 1924). In Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg (see there) [BZF]: Ja. nos from Djember (1924).
