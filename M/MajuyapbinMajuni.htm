Majuyap bin Majuni

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1912, Jesselton, Br. N. Borneo.

 

career:

Was appointed Forest Scaler in Br. N. Borneo in 1940; resigned in 1947 and later employed in the Agricultural Department.

 

collections:

He collected at Sandakan and Jesselton, Br. N. Borneo, cf. also sub Forest Department British North Borneo [SAN]; at least partly numbered S.H.
