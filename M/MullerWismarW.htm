Müller(-Wismar), Dr Wilhelm

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Died: before 1927.

 

career:

Ethnologist and linguist who accompanied some German Southsea expeditions, including the well-known ‘Südsee-Expedition der Hamburgische Wissenschaftliche Stiftung’ in the ‘Peiho’, 1908-10 (cf. also sub G. Duncker).

 

collections:

Possibly he collected a few specimens together with F.E. Hellwig (see there).

 

biographical data:

Portr. in Thilenius, Ergebn. Südsee-Exp. 1908-10, 1. Allg., Hamburg 1927, pl. 1.
