Manner, Harley Ichiro

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1941, Honolulu, Hawaii, U.S.A.

 

career:

Geographer, B.A. University of Hawaii, currently (1968) working on M.A. and Ph.D. in Geography at Hawaii University. He accompanied Dr John M. Street (see there) as Research Assistant to New Guinea, to gather data on morphology, structure, and floristics of the savannah and forest vegetation. Publications will follow.

 

Collecting localities:

Terr. of New Guinea (Mar. 1967-Feb. 1968): in the vicinity of Kupeng, Kompiai, Koinambe and Tsuenkai of the Jimi Valley, Western Highlands District.

 

collections:

Nos 1-599 in Herb. Leiden [L], Lae [LAE], and in Hawaii Geogr. Dept.
