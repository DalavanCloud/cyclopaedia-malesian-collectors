Moysey, Louis (or Lewis)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Came to the Malay Peninsula from India, and now has left; amateur botanist.

Licuala moyseyi Furtado was named after him.

 

Collecting localities:

Malay Peninsula: near Kota Tinggi, Johore (c. 1935-36); G. Padang in Trengganu (c. May 1937).1

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING], from G. Padang collected together with Kiah (see there). Probably in Herb. Harvard Unity. (Arnold Arbor. [A], Gray [GH], and Farlow) too.1 Plants Mal. Peninsula (1937) with Kiah bin Salleh also in Herb. Edinb. [E].

 

literature:

(1) cf. Chron. Bot. 4, 1938,p.251.
