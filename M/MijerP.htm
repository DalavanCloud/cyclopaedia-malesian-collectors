Mijer, Pieter

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1812, Batavia, Java. Died: 1881, Scheveningen, Z.H., Holland.

 

career:

From 1866-72 Governor-General of the D.E.I.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: material of Rafflesia patma Blume; Hort. Bog.: living specimens of ‘Widjojo Koesomo’, both collected on a voyage through Java in 1868.

 

biographical data:

Levensber. Mij d. Ned. Letterk. 1884, p. 71; Encyclop. N.I. 2, 1918.
