Meer, Pieter van der

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1908, Rotterdam, Holland.

 

career:

School-teacher who came to Java in 1931; for 3 years stationed at Batavia, 3 years at Tjimahi, and subsequently at Batavia again. After World War II evacuated to Holland, living at Rotterdam; at present back at Batavia (see sub Hoed).

 

Collecting localities:

1939-41. W. Java: Islands in the Bay ofBatavia: Purmerend and Kerkhof; Moeara Heemraad; environs of Batavia. 

 

collections:

Partly together with G. den Hoed (see there, also sub liter.); some dupl. in Herb. Bog. [BO]. Private Herb.: c. 800 nos, mainly weeds. Occasionally he presented specimens to Herb. Leiden [L].
