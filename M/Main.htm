career:

Indonesian clerk of the Buitenzorg Herbarium. In May-June 1949 he assisted Kostermans on an expedition to Morotai (Moluccas). In the second half of 1949 he assisted Miss Dr Polak (see there) in collecting plants in West Borneo. See also under Djamhari.
