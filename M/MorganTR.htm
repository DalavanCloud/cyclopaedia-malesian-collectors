Morgan, Tom R.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

A soils officer of the Papua and New Guinea Department of Agriculture. In 1968 living at Surrey Hills, Vict., Australia.

 

Collecting localities:

He made a few collections in E. New Guinea, mainly herbaceous plants, in the 1950s.

 

collections:

In Herb. Lae [LAE].
