Main, T. Wilson

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1879. Died: 1944.

 

career:

Came from the Botanic Gardens, Glasgow, in 1901 to Kew Gardens as a Student Gardener; in 1903 subkeeper in the Arboretum; left for the Malay Peninsula in 1906 as Superintendent at Batu Tiga, Selangor Rubber Centres; in charge of the Hill Garden, Taiping, 1907; Assistant Singapore Botanic Gardens, Apr. 1908-May 1910; subsequently on a rubber estate once more. He returned to England in 1920; employed as Estates Bailiff Tregenna Park Hotel St Ives, Cornwall from 1922-31; in charge of Finsbury Borough Parks etc., London, from 1935.

 

collections:

He collected in the Malay Peninsula; in Herb. Sing. [SING]; Herb. Perak Mus.: 66 nos (pres. 1906), incorporated in Singapore now [SING].

 

biographical data:

Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5.
