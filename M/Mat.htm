Mat

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A plant collector in Singapore, 1888-97, who accompanied Lake & Kelsall (see those) in 1892, visited G. Pulai (Johore; probably with Lake in 1892), and accompanied Becher (see there) up the Tahan River (Pahang) in 1893 (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5).

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]: plants from the Malay Peninsula.
