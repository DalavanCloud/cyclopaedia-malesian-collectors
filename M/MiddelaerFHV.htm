Middelaer, Frederik Hendrik Victor

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1890, Djokjakarta, Java. Died: 1953, Australia.

 

career:

Entered the D.E. Indian Government service in 1914, in the course of years filling various administrative posts, mostly at Buitenzorg; finally Sub-Director of the Department of Economic Affairs.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: Epipogium roseum from G. Boender (Salak) in West Java (coll. June 1935).
