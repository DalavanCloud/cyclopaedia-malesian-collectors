Mactier, Dr W.F.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

collections:

Herb. Edinburgh [E]: ferns from P. Penang.

He is cited as the collector of Asplenium mactieri Bedd. in that island (cf. Journ. Bot. 26, 1888, p. 3). His ferns were collected in 1865.
