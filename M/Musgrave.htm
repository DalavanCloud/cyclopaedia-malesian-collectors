Musgrave, Mrs

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

? wife of A. Musgrave, Government Secretary of P. Moresby, SE. New Guinea.

Endotrichella musgraveae was probably named after her.1

 

collections:

Mosses from Brit. New Guinea = Papua (1897), in Herb. Binstead (= Kew [K]).2 Up till now no evidence of collections of phanerogams.
