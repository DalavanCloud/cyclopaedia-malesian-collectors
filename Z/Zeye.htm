Zeye, Captain Lieutenant

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Of S.M.S. ‘Gazelle’ (for detailed data on this expedition cf. sub Naumann).

Probably the collector Zeyl (see there) is identical, the name being misread.

 

Collecting localities:

Is cited by Engler (in Engl. Bot. Jahrb. 7, 1886, p. 459) as the collector of a plant near Koepang, Timor. The expedition visited the island in May 1875.
