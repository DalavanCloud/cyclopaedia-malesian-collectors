Correct for J.F.H. Zieck (see Suppl. Cycl. Fl. Mal. I, 5, 1958, p. cccxxxvi).

 

career:

In 1962 he left New Guinea. In 1968 working for the Forest Products Research Centre, Boroko, Port Moresby, Papua.

Author of several mimeographed internal reports on the forests of New Guinea.

 

Collecting localities:

Borneo and West New Guinea. Reconnaissance of the Prafi Plain, W. of Manokwari about 1952.

 

collections:

Cf. sub Boswezen Nieuw Guinea [MAN].
