Zurowetz, J.E.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Owner of an orchid nursery at Sebidang, Sambas (NW. Borneo) from c. 1905 onwards (according to Saheb of the Singapore Bot. Gard.).

Coelogyne zurowetzii Carr was named after him (cf. Backer, Verkl. Woordenb., 1936).

 

Collecting localities:

Sebidang, Sambas (NW. Borneo) from c. 1905 onwards.

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]: specimens of a number of local Bornean orchids which he sent to Singapore for naming.
