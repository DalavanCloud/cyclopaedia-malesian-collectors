Zwickey, A. Lynn

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Collecting localities:

Made an expedition for the Arnold Arboretum (Jamaica Plain) and the Bot. Gard. Michigan, to investigate the flora of the Prov. of Lanao, Mindanao, P.I., in 1938.

 

collections:

> 776 nos; Herb. Arn. Arbor. [A], GRAY Herb. [GH], and Michigan [MICH]; printed labels!; 214 dupl. in Herb. N.Y. Bot. Gard. [NY] (acq. 1942).

The collection included the new species Isoetes philippinensis Merr. & L.M.Perry.1

 

literature:

(1) E.D. Merrill & L.M. Perry: ‘A new Philippine Isoetes’ (Amer. Fern Journ. 30, 1940, p. 18-20).
