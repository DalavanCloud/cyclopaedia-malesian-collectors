Garai

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Was a Saribas Dyak who had been years at the Mission School and wrote well. According to Haviland he was industrious and intelligent and the former taught him botany as far as time permitted. Collector employed by G.D. Haviland (see there).

 

collections:

Cited by Bloembergen (cf. Blumea 1, 1935, p. 265) as the collector of a no 2018 on Mt Buan, Sarawak (NW. Borneo), material of which preserved in Kew [K] and Sarawak [SAR]; cf. also sub Garaman.
