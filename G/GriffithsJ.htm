Griffiths, John

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Author of a paper on worm-shells, discovered at an island off the NW. coast of Sumatra (Phil. Trans. vol. 96); at the time in the possession of a beautiful collection of corallines. Evidently about 1800 a resident of Sumatra.1

 

collections:

He sent living material from Sumatra to the Calcutta Bot. Gard.2

 

literature:

(1) Cf. W. Marsden: ‘The History of Sumatra’ (1811, p. 121, 122).

(2) Cf. Roxburgh, Hort. Beng. 1814.
