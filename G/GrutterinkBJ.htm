Grutterink, Barend Johannes

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1886, Wageningen, Gld, Holland.

 

career:

Forest Officer, since 1913 in the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service; at first stationed at Buitenzorg, since 1914 in the teak districts of Java; in 1925 transferred to Soekaboemi, in 1929 for some months at Buitenzorg, and subsequently to Magelang (Centr. Java), in Aug. 1930 to Malang, in 1931 to Bandoeng; in 1932 he was appointed Inspector of Forests; in 1934 he retired and returned to Holland.

 

Collecting localities:

1918-26. E. Java: Kediri.-1928. W. Java: Soekaboemi.-1929. Centr. Java: Poerworedjo.-1930. Centr. Java: G. Andong (July).-1932. W. Java.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]; collection from Kediri (1918) numbered in the series Beumée (see there). Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now BO]: 1 no from Kediri; Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: several Ja. nos.
