Grijp, A. de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1898, Papendrecht, Z.H., Holland.

 

career:

Since 1921 ‘Boscharchitect’; in the D.E. Indian Forest Service; stationed successively in the teak districts of Java, in Timor (Koepang) (Dec. 1923-1930), for a short time in Bali (Singaradja), and in Nov. 1930 transferred to Atjeh (Langsa) in N. Sumatra, in July 1931 once more to Timor (Koepang), from Sept. 1938-39 in Celebes (Makassar and Poso), and in 1940 in Lombok (Mataram).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: nos 1-6 (= F.R.I. bb. nos 21416-21421) from Timor (pres. Oct. 1936 through the intermediary of the Forest Research Institute at Buitenzorg) and other dupl. F.R.I. in Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: from Timor (1924, 1935-37 coll.).
