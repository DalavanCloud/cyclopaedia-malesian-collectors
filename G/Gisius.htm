Gisius

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Owner of the ‘Smeroehoeve’, a primitive hotel formerly situated near Ranoe Pani on the saddle of G. Tengger and Smeroe.

He brought together large collections of natural history objects.

 

Collecting localities:

E. Java, principally from G. Smeroe-Tengger.

 

collections:

Private herbarium. In Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 48 dupl. (1921-30); Herb. Bog. [BO]: 25 nos G. Smeroe (1941); dupl. in Herb. Leiden [L]  too. It seems doubtful whether his private herbarium is still intact.
