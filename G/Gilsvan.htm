Career

 

Gils, van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A resident of Padang, Sumatra West Coast, sent plants to Buitenzorg in 1873.

He may be identical with A. van Gils, a member of the ‘Kon. Natuurkundige Vereeniging’ (Batavia), president of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry at Padang, since 1866 Consular Agent of France.
