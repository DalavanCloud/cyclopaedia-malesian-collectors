Gors, Walter

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Of Port Moresby, SE. New Guinea, agent of the firm of Burns, Philp & Co. He accompanied G.R. le Hunte (see there) on his tour to Mt Werirata and the Rauna (= Rona) Falls in June 1899. Cf. also sub W.H. Goss ( ? identical).

 

collections:

Herb. Sydney [NSW] (pres. through Burns, Philp & Co. in 1902).
