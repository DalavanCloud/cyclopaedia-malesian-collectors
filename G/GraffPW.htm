Graff, Paul Weidemeyer

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1880, Indianapolis, U.S.A.

 

career:

Mycologist who took his Dr’s degree in 1932; from 1911-14 on the staff of the Bureau of Science at Manila, Luzon, P.I.; later at Montana, Columbia, etc.; since 1931 Consulting Mycologist.

 

collections:

Mostly Fungi, numbered in the Bur. Sci. series (see there), Herb. Manila [PNH].

 

biographical data:

Amer. Men of Sci. 1938.
