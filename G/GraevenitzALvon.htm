Graevenitz, Miss A.L. von

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

And Miss E. Stein (see there) stayed in Java in 1907 to study for a few months the tropical flora and to collect material.1

In 1921 a certain Dr Luise von Graevenitz, Assistant at the Institute for Genetical Research of het Agricultural College, Berlin, died (cf. Ber. D. B.G. 39, 1921, p. 63); she may be identical with the collector.

 

collections:

In Herb. Bog. [BO]: Rhododendron zollingeri J.J.Sm. from Mt Diëng, Centr. Java.2 We do not have data on the location of contingent collections.
