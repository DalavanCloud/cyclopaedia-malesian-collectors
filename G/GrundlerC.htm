Gründler, C.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Assistant of J. Elbert (see there) on the so-called Sunda Expedition, 1909-10.

Peperomia gruendleri C.DC. was named after him.

 

Collecting localities & collections:

 cf. sub Elbert (also for liter., etc.). Probably the itinerary is not completely identical, so Gründler alone collected on G. Tambora (Sumbawa) between 1000 and 1300 m alt.1

 

literature:

(1) cf. Elbert: ‘Die Sunda-Expedition’ 2, 1912, p. 101.
