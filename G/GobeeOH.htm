Gobée, Oscar Hugo

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1879, o/b s.s. Overijssel, in the Channel.

 

career:

Forest Officer, since 1903 in D.E. Indian Government service; retired in 1928.

 

collections:

He collected a plant on G. Midangan, Banjoemas, Centr. Java (Oct. 27, 1925), no 6686 of series Beumée (see there); in Herb. Bog. [BO].
