Giltay, Eduard

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1858, Rotterdam, Holland. Died: 1935, Apeldoorn, Gld, Holland.

 

career:

Professor at the Agricultural College,Wageningen; he was granted the Dutch Buitenzorg Fund, for which he stayed in Java from Sept. 1895 until Jan. 1896.1

 

collections:

Herb. Wageningen [WAG]: material collected at Buitenzorg and Tjibodas in W. Java, provided with notes on the occurrence of stomata.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Dmmerman in Ann. Jard. Bot. Buit. 45, 1935, p. 20.

E. Giltay: ‘Ueber die vegetabilische Stoffbildung in den Tropen und in Mitteleuropa’ (Ann. Jard. Bot. Buit. 15, 1898, p. 43-72, pl. 11-12).

 

biographical data:

Stud. Alman. Wageningen 1912, p. 49-50 + portr.; Landbouw 10, 1935, p. 413-414 + portr.; Landb. Tijdschr. 48, 1936, p. 417-420 + portr.; Chron. Bot. 2, 1936, p. 237 (portr.)-238; Wie is dat? ed. 1-3.
