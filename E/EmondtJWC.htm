Emondt, Johannes Wilhelmus Constantinus

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1875, Batavia, Java. Died: 1921, Lawang, Java.

 

career:

Since 1900 in the employ of the Opium Police; in 1912 appointed Head Overseer in the D.E.I. Forest Service, for many years stationed in Atjeh (N. Sumatra). He resigned in Oct. 1919 on account of illness.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: especially Dipterocarpaceae, from Peureula Distr. in Atjeh, N. Sumatra (early part of 1914), etc., bearing low numbers. Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: plants from Singkel and Langsa, Atjeh, N. Sumatra (coll. 1914-15).1

 

literature:

(1) cf. Typed report on the Peureula exploration 1915 in For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg.
