Collecting localities

 

Eddy, Alan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Collecting localities:

In charge of the mosses in the British Museum, joined an expedition to the Finisterre Mts etc. in 1964-65 (see sub A.C. Jermy and others). He was to collect mosses, liverworts, and lichens, but might have collected some phanerogams.

In 1969 he accompanied Dr A.C. Jermy on an expedition to Central Celebes (Latimodjong Mts) and S. New Ireland (Bismarck Archipelago).
