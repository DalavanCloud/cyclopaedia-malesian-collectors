Eibergen

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career + collections:

Scheffer described a new species, Tabernaemontana pentasticta, ‘in insulis Aroë Eibergen lecta, nobiscum a van der Goes, nunc in horto floret’ (cf. Nat. Tijdschr. N.I. 31, 1870, p. 22-23); Hort. Bog.

A certain H.C. van Eijbergen was for several years an official in Ambon (in 1852 Captain in the army, in 1861 District Officer); in Jan. 1868 he was appointed Assistant Resident at Boeleleng, Bali. He may be identical with the collector.
