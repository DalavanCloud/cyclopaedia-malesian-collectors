career:

Philippine Ranger, in the employ of the Forest Dept Br. N. Borneo (see there), Sandakan. Is also credited with collecting in the Philippines, evidently prewar.

 

collections:

Herb. Manila [PNH] (destroyed); dupl. in Herb. Bog. [BO] (pres. 1929); also dupl. in Herb. Arn. Arbor. [A], New York [NY].
