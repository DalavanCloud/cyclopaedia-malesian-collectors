Career

 

Espiritu, Rogelio A.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1938, Sta. Cruz, Manila, Philippines.

 

career:

Joined the National Museum, Philippine National Herbarium, in 1960 as Botany-Aide. From 1963 onwards he accompanied H.G. Gutierrez, D.R. Mendoza, P.A. Cordero, R. del Rosario, M. Jacobs, and E.J. Reynoso on collecting tours in the Philippines. No field numbers of his own.
