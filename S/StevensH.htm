Collections

 

Stevens, H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

collections:

Is cited as the collector of Burmannia disticha L. in Mowless Distr., Mt Misim (E. New Guinea) (cf. Jonker, Monograph of the Burmanniaceae, Thesis, Utrecht 1938, p. 120). The said specimen in Gray Herb., Cambr. (Mass.) [GH].

The Stevens mentioned in the Cyclopaedia (Fl. Mal. Vol. 1) as a collector in Mowless District, E. New Guinea, collected in Morobe Distr. on Mt Mis(s)im in 1932-33. On the typed labels of the Gray Herb. it wrongly reads Mowlee Distr.!
