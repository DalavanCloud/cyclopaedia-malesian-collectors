Schmuziger, André Werner

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1899, Aarau, Switzerland.

 

career:

Agriculturist, educated at Zürich University, where he took his Ph. Dr’s degree in 1935. After attending the course of the Sugar School at Amsterdam, he came to Java as Group-Adviser in the employ of the Experiment Station for the Java Sugar Industry, and was successively stationed at Banjoemas (1925-28), and Djombang (1928-32). Since 1937 in the employ of the Potassium Syndicate in Europe.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 5 nos of plants from the neighbourhood of Djombang and from the s.f. Tjoeker near Soerabaja, E. Java (coll. in 1931).
