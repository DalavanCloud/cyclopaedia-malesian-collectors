Sappi bin Hassan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Foreman Gardener, Botanic Gardens, Singapore (1922-38). Probably Sappan bin Hassan.

 

collections:

He may have brought in odd specimens from Singapore Island, but never made dried collections. His name may appear on specimens in the Sing. Herbarium of cultivated plants [SING].
