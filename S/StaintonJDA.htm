Stainton, John David Adam

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1921, London, England.

 

career:

Was educated at Winchester College and Christchurch Oxford. Hons degrees in History and Law; barrister-at-Law. Assistant in the organization of the Royal Society North Borneo Expedition 1961. Plants were collected with Chew and Corner (see there) in the RSNB series. His real interest is Himalayan, but he collected in the Balkan and Greenland too.

 

Collecting localities:

For Borneo see under Corner.
