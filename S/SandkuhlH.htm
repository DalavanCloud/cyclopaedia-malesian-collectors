Collections

 

Sandkuhl, H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Herb. Manila [PNH]: Philippine plants (pres. 1915-16); partly with private numbers, but also in the B.S. (cf. Bureau of Science) and F.B. (cf. Forestry Bureau) series. Many of his plants are cited in Merrill, Enum. Philip. Fl. Pl.; evidently the numbers exceed 300. According to Merrill he sold part of his duplicates.
