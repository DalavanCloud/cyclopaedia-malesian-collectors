Sybrandi, J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Died: c. 1940, Aerdenhout, Holland

 

career:

Manager of the sugar plantation Soemberhardjo, discovered Amorphophallus oncophyllus at Lebak Barang, Centr. Java (coll. Jan. 12, 1918).1

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: some plants collected at Lebak Barang, Pekalongan in Centr. Java (Aug. 1917).

 

literature:

(1) J. Sybrandi: ‘Op zoek naar een Amorphophallus’ (Trop. Natuur 8, 1919, p. 33-41, 5 fig.).
