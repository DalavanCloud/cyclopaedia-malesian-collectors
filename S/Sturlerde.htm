Collections

 

Sturler, de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Possibly different collectors bearing the same family name sent living plants to Hort. Bog. The following plants were presented: living plants in 1877-78 by de Sturler of Tjiomas Estate near Buitenzorg (W. Java), an orchid by a de Sturler of Tandjong Pinang (Riouw Arch.) in 1898, living orchids from the Lampong Districts (S. Sumatra) (possibly from V. de Sturler, who made a hunting trip in these districts in 1914).
