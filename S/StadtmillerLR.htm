Stadtmiller, L.R.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Forest Officer, later Professor at Michigan, worked for some time in the Buitenzorg Herbarium in 1921.

 

Collecting localities:

1921. Sumatra East Coast: at Bandar Poelau (July 6), Tabat Pandjang (Sept. 27), Kota Malintang (Sept. 29), Moeara Bahar (Oct. 15), Senawar.-Together with Ferraris he collected in Mindanao, possibly in 1921 too.

 

collections:

Few collections. Lam (in Bull. Jard. Bot. Buit. sér. 3, vol. 7, 1925) mentions several Palaquiums collected by him in Sumatra East Coast, some of the plants numbered above 100. The Palaquium collected by him in Mindanao is numbered in the F.B. (= Forestry Bureau, Manila) series, Herb. Manila [PNH].
