Schwarz, Eduard

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: 1862, Vienna, Austria.

 

career:

Naval Surgeon who was to undertake the botany department of the expedition in the ‘Novara’ (1857-59) with Jelinek (see there and sub Hochstetter). According to Wawra von Fernsee1 he embarked as a botanist, but it was soon evident that he was none! Notwithstanding he did good work as a medical observer, and reporter on the voyage.

 

literature:
