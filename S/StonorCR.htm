Collecting localities:

Collected Rhododendrons in Hagen and Bismarck Ranges, NE. New Guinea during World War II.1 In 1949 he again collected on Mt Wilhelm (Bismarck Mts).

 

collections:

> 13 nos; in Herb. Edinb. [E].
