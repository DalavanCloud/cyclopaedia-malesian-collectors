Soehanda

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Indonesian, then in the employ of the Buitenzorg Herbarium, who attended the expedition of  J.K. de Jong (see there) to the Lesser Sunda Islands.

 

Collecting localities:

1937. With J.K. de Jong Expedition. Flores (June 6-30): Laboe(h)an Badjo (arriving the 6th), Emborak ( = Emboera or Mboera) (9-11), Nanganai (l 1-12), Kenari (14), Emborak and Nanganai (16), Gorontalo (17), Paroegaboleng (17-19), Sibinongko (20). kp. Aer Kemiring (21), P. Badjo (22), Boibaroe (23), Gololangkas (24), Tjopi (25), Laboe(h)an Badjo (25), P. Papagaran Besar (July 1); P. Komodo (3-6); Flores: Laboe(h)an Badjo-Reo (8-11, no collecting done).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 363 nos, mainly from Flores; the nos 275-285 from P. Papagaran Besar, and nos 286-363 from P. Komodo.
