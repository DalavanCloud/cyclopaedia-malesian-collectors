Saänam (or Saänan)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Student ‘mantri’ in the Botanic Gardens at Buitenzorg, Java, who attended the van Hulstijn (see there and below) Expedition to the Moluccas.

 

Collecting localities:

van Hulstijn Exp., 1913-14. For the first part of this expedition cf. sub Atje(h). From May 1914 in the Obi Islands; the operations were discontinued in connexion with the European war. The plants from Ambon were probably collected during the voyage home. Saänam was back at Buitenzorg towards the end of the year.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: plants from van Hulstijn Exp., viz 141 nos from Obi, P. Bisa and Ambon.
