Died: Dec. 5, 1949, Penang General Hospital, Penang, Malaya.

 

career:

Arrived from Ceylon in Malaya. Joined the Forest Department Malay Peninsula in Dec. 1907 as a Forest Ranger; retired in 1948 as S.A. Conservator of Forests;  

 

Collecting localities:

Collected mainly in Perak and Pahang.

 

collections:

In Herb. Kuala Lump. [KEP], cf, sub Conservator of Forests series, Kepong.

 

biographical data:
