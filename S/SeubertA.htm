Seubert, Ad.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A Forest Officer who collected mosses, especially on Dieng Plateau in Centr. Java. He is probably identical with the later Inspector of Forests of that name.1

The mosses Entodon seubertianus Fleisch. and Ectropothecium seuberti Fleisch. were named after him; the latter species was collected near Wonosobo (= Diëng Plateau), probably the former too.2

 

collections:

Herb. Berl. [B]: Java mosses (coll. in 1879). Up till the present we have no data that he collected also phanerogams.

 

literature:

(1) The Inspector Seubert is the author of: ‘Natuurlijke reboisatie op het Moeriagebergte in de residentie Japara’ (Tijdschr. Binnenl. Bestuur 19, 1900, p. 498-529).

(2) cf. M. Fleischer, Die Musci der Flora von Buitenzorg, vol. 4, p. 1153 and 1397.
