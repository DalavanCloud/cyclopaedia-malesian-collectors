Sario, Inocencio H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1928, Bagabag, Nueva Vizcaya, Luzon, P.I.

 

career:

A graduate of the Forest School, University of the Philippines (B.Sc.For.). Appointed as Forest Ranger in 1951, stationed at Kretam, North Borneo Timber Company logging operations; in 1954 transferred to Kudat.

 

Collecting localities:

At Kretam, Sandakan, Br. N. Borneo.

 

collections:

Cf. sub Forest Department British North Borneo [SAN].
