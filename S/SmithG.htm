Smith, Gordon

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1941, Aberfeldy, Scotland

 

career:

Was educated at Aberdeen University (B.Sc. Hons 1963, Ph.D. 1968). From 1968 Lecturer, School of Biology, University of Malaya, Kuala Lumpur.

 

Collecting localities:

1968. West Malaysia, mainly in Selangor, Pahang, and Perak States. 1969. With E. Soepadmo in Borneo.

 

collections:

Nos 150-400 in Herb. Aberd. Univ. [ABD]
