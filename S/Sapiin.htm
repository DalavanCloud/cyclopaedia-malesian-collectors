Sapiïn

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Indonesian collector.

 

Collecting localities:

Collected in W. Java, especially on Poentjak Pass and at Tjibodas (slope G. Gedeh), as early as 1896. Most of his collecting was done in the years 1914-24 on the N. slope of G. Gedeh.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO], partly numbered in Beumée’s series (see there); dupl. in Herb. Leiden [L].
