Died: Jan. 25, 1971.

 

career:

Sakai Forest Guard, joined in 1929 as tree climber and Herbarium collector at the Forest Research Institute, Kepong. Was also in Sabah, Sarawak, and Brunei; retired in March 1969 after 42 years service as plant collector at Kepong (Malaya).
