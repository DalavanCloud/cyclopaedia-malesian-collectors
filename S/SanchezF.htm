Sanchez, Father F.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Of the ‘Ateneo de Manila’; a very amateurish collector.

Canarium sanchezii Merr. was named after him.

 

collections:

Herb. Manila [PNH]: 19 Philippine plants (pres. 1911/12) and 24 Benguet plants (Luzon) for identification (1912/13). At all events he collected in Mindanao1 and Cebu2 too.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Philip. Journ. Sci. C. Bot. 11, 1916, p. 183.

(2) Plants from there cited in Merrill, Enum. Philip. Fl. Pl.
