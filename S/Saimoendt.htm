Saimoendt

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Indonesian plant collector who attended an expedition in Sumatra (see below).

 

Collecting localities:

1925. Djambi Expedition in Centr. Sumatra (July-Oct.). Detailed itiner. etc. see sub O. Posthumus; in the month of September Saimoendt collected in Ds. Baroe, at Sg. Karing, Sg. Merangin, etc.-W. Java: G. Moenara(h) and G. Noentjoeng on Roempin Estate, NW of Buitenzorg (2nd half of Dec.).1

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: Sumatra plants Djambi Expedition, partly collected by Saimoendt and numbered in the series of Posthumus (labels also bearing private numbers); nos 1-63 from Roempin Estate.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Versl. Pl. Tuin Buitenzorg for 1925, p. 7.
