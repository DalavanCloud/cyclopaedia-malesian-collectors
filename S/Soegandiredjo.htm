Soegandi(redjo)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Indonesian in the employ of the Buitenzorg Herbarium till 1910, finally in the rank of Assistant Mantri; later clerk of the market, and landing in jail on account of theft.

In 1900 he collected on behalf of Koorders, and in the same year he attended Boerlage and Smith on their voyage to the Moluccas.

 

Collecting localities:

W. Java. 1900. Near Tjibeureum (March 12), Kota Batoe (Apr. 9), Depok (May 28), G. Salak, etc.-1909. Priangan Res. (staying 3 weeks on Taloen Estate near Bandoeng): G. Kentjana near Taloen (Apr. 22), G. Windoe (24), Taloen (25), G. Kembang (27), G. Malabar (29).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: > 156 nos from G. Malabar, > 115 nos from Taloen, etc. Some labels of his have no indication as to the name of the collector, but are provided with the initials ‘G.W.’, standing for the collecting locality G. Windoe!
