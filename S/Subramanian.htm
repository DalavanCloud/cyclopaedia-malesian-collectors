Subramanian

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Foreman Gardener employed by the Singapore Botanic Gardens from c. 1918-32.

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]: odd collections made in Singapore Island (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, nos 4-5). According to Mr Holttum his name may also appear as collector of plant specimens in the Gardens.
