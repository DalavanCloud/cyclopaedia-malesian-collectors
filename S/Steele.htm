Steele, Captain

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

According to Wichmann (in Entd. Gesch. N.G., Nova Guinea 22, p. 604), Steele left Port Moresby for Brisbane in 1895.

 

collections:

Of the ‘Ivanhoe’ brought Dendrobium viridiflorum Baill. from New Guinea (cf. Bailey in Queensl. Agric. Journ. 1898, p. 285); specimen probably in Herb. Brisbane [BRI].
