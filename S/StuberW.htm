Stüber, W.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Professional collector, settled near Hollandia in Dutch North New Guinea. In the years 1930-34 he collected numerous insects, especially Odonata, on behalf of Lieftinck, Buitenzorg Museum. Dealer in living New Guinea orchids.

 

collections:

Herb. For. Res. lnst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: 1 no from Hollandia, North New Guinea (pres. 1927).
