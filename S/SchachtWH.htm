Schacht, Walter Holland

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1911, Gympie, Queensland, Australia.

 

career:

Forest Ranger, collected at Sogeri and in Misima Island, Papua, after 1945. Later resigned from the Administration of Papua and New Guinea.

 

collections:

In the N.G.F. series (cf. New Guinea Forces) [LAE].
