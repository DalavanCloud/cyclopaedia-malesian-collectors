Sigafoos, Corporal Robert S. (or ? A.)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Botanist, graduate from Ohio State University, collected during World War II, W of Hollandia, N of Simboro Strait of Sentani Lake, Dutch N. New Guinea, in Febr. 1945. Subsequently he collected in the Philippines. Now at Harvard University, Cambr., Mass.

 

collections:

Herb. Arn. Arbor. [A]: c. 100 spec-imens from New Guinea.
