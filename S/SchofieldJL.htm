Schofield, J.L.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Genetist employed at Chemara Plantation, a private research station belonging to Messrs Guthrie & Co. In June 1953 he was no longer in Malaya.

 

Collecting localities:

He collected at least a specimen of Cassia surattensis Burm.f. at Layang Layang (Johore), Malay Peninsula.

 

collections:

In Herb. Sing. [SING]. Although the specimen was not numbered, he might incidentally have collected other plants.
