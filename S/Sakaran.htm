Sakaran (Sakaran or Sekarang)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Student ‘mantri’ of the Botanic Gardens at Buitenzorg, who attended Nieuwenhuis’s expedition to Borneo in the years 1898-99 (see sub Nieuwenhuis). Since 1904 appointed ‘mantri’; retired in 1926.

 

collections:

Hort. Bog.: living plants from Borneo,1 and Tjibodas (G. Gedeh, W. Java; pres. 1910).

 

literature:

(1) cf. Versl. Pl. Tuin Buitenzorg for 1899, p. 66-67.
