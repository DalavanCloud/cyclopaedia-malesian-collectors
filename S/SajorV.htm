Sajor, Valentin

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1893, Cabugao, Ilocos Sur, Luzon, P.I.

 

career:

From 1917-18 Ranger-Scaler of the Bureau of Forestry of the Philippines; since 1924 Officer in charge Bureau of Forestry; B.S.F. University Idaho in 1926, and M.F. Yale University in 1927; from 1928-30 District Forester; from 1930-33 Assistant Chief Div. of Licenses and 1933-34 of the Div. of Forestry & Range; since 1934 Forester in the Bureau of Forestry and Assistant Professor of Range Management, Manila.

Author of some sylvicultural papers.1

 

collections:

Herb. Manila [PNH], numbered in the F.B. (= Forestry Bureau, see there) series.

 

literature:

(1) V. Sajor: ‘Forests and forestry in the Philippines’ (Makiling Echo 6, 1927, p. 2-17), and some others in the same periodical.

 

biographical data:

Nat. Res. Counc. P.I. Bull. 7, 1935, p. 888, incl. bibliogr.
