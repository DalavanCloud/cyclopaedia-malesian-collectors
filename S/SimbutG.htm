Simbut, Gabriel

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1923, Penampang, Jesselton, Br. N. Borneo.

 

career:

Was employed by the Forest Service in Br. N. Borneo from 1948 onwards; graduate of the Forest School, Kepong, in 1949; in 1955 stationed at Weston as Forest Ranger.

 

Collecting localities:

Most of his collections made at Sandakan and Labuk.

 

collections:

Cf. Forest Department British North Borneo [SAN].
