Spangler

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

R. Brown in Bennett & Brown, Plant. Jav. rarior., cites plants collected by Spangler in Java. Miquel already supposed the collector to be Spanoghe;1 this was later confirmed by Schumann.2

Thus the name Sterculia spangleri R.Br. was erroneously given (cf. Backer, Verkl. Woordenb., 1936).

 

literature:

(1) cf. Flora Ind. Bat. 12, p. 174.

(2) cf. Engl. Bot. Jahrb. 24, Beibl. 58, p. 17.
