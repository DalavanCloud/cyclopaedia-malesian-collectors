Seto, Ko

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Pteridologist and a member of a joint expedition of the Osaka Museum of Natural History and the National Museum, Manila, in the Philippines. 

 

Collecting localities:

They collected on Mt Apo in Mindanao (Davao) from Nov. 19-Dec. 20, 1969.1

 

collections:

Shared equally between Osaka Mus. [OSA] and Herb. Nat. Mus. Manila [PNH].

 

literature:

(1) Cf. Fl. Mal. Bull. no 25, 1971, p.1891-1892.
