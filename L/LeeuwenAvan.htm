Leeuwen, Arie van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1915, Meerkerk, Z.H., Netherlands.

 

career:

Educated at Wageningen Agricultural College. In September 1956 he went to Dutch New Guinea (Hollandia) as Inspector of the Veterinary Department.

 

Collecting localities:

1957. W. New Guinea: Kebar Valley (Oct. 25); Kabarai, Waigeo(u) (Oct.-Nov.); Kofiau I., W of New Guinea (Nov. 22).-1958. W. New Guinea: Baliem Valley (Mar.).

 

collections:

He collected on behalf of the Forestry Service; Herb. Manokwari [MAN] and Leiden [L].
