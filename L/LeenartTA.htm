Leenart, Theodoor Alphons

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1907, Mr Cornelis, Java. Died: between 1942-45, in a Japanese prisoner camp.

 

career:

Attended the Colonial School of Agriculture at Deventer, Holland; since 1929 Assistant Pedologist (Bodemkundig ambtenaar) at the Institute for Soil Science, Buitenzorg.

 

Collecting localities:

1941. SE. Borneo: Pleihari District, between Djorong and Kintap in . open savannahs (May 13-20), near Assem River; E. Java: Bodjonegoro Residency (Aug. 6-Sept. 4), in teak forests etc.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 30 Borneo plants and 89 (nos 31-119) from Java.
