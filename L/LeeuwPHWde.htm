Leeuw, Pieter Helenus Wilhelmus de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1891, Barneveld, Gld, Holland.

 

career:

Since 1914 Forest Officer in D.E. Indian Government service; stationed in Java, except in the years 1926-28 when he was at Pontianak in W. Borneo.

 

Collecting localities:

W. Borneo (1928). -W. Java: Indramajoe, ‘siïl’ (grass) plains (c. 1934).

 

collections:

Spathoglottis affinis de VR. from Indramajoe (Java) in Herb. Bog. [BO]. Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF] (see there) : 117 Borneo nos (in the bb. series).
