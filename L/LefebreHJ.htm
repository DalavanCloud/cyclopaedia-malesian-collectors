Lefèbre, H.(J.)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Curator of the Mountain Botanic Garden at Tjibodas, W. Java, from Sept. 1889-Sept. 1893 (temporarily relieved at his own request from Febr.-June 1892); towards the end of 1893 he was transferred to the Botanic Gardens, Buitenzorg.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: > 112 nos, including ferns, from Tjibodas and vicinity (coll. 1889-93); dupl. in Herb. Leiden [L]. He evidently collected 29 nos of mosses too.1

 

literature:

(1) J. Cardot: ‘Note sur deux collections de Mousses de l’Archipel Indien’ (Rev. Bryol. 1901, p. 112-117).
