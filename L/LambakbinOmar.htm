Lambak bin Omar

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Ranger in the Forest Department, Malay Peninsula, since 1903; now retired.

 

Collecting localities:

Malay Peninsula: in Pahang, Rumpin; Kuantan (1931); mainly in E. Pahang.

 

collections:

Herb. Kuala Lump. [KEP], numbered in the C.F. (see sub Conservator of Forests) series. Evidently duplicates at Kew [K] and Singapore [SING].
