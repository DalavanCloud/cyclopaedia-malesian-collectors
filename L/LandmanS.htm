Collections

 

Landman, Sylvany

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Gathered plants at Doole on the island of Flores (Lesser Sunda Isls) on Nov. 13-14, 1679. The specimens are with Herb. Petiver & Sloane in the Brit. Mus. [BM], they are mostly referred to Ray. He must be identical with Sylvanus Landon (see below), who is cited to have collected in Flores.
