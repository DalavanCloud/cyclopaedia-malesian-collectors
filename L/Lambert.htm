Lambert

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is cited as the collector of plants in the Moluccas in 1816,1 which are said to be preserved in Herb. Decand. (Geneva [G]), and of a no 135 from Singapore.2 The plants mentioned are probably ex Herb. A.B. Lambert, but not collected by himself. Part of the latter’s herbarium is preserved in the Brit. Mus. [BM] (purch. in 1842; mainly coll. Ruiz & Pavon from America);3 and in Herb. Deless. (Geneva [G]). ‘Catalogue of the sale’ in Brit. Museum and at Kew [K].

 

literature:

(1) cf. Pflanzenreich 46, p. 291

(2) cf. l.c. 103, 1938, p. 279.

(3) cf. The History of the collections ... of the British Museum’ 1, 1904, p. 87.
