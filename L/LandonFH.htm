Landon, F.H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1909, U.K. Died: Sept. 1956.

 

career:

Was educated in forest­ry at Oxford University (B.A.); from 1932 onwards a member of the Malayan Forest Service; later Chief Research Officer in the Forest Research Institute at Kepong, Selangor.

 

collections:

He occasionally collected in the C.F. series (cf. Conservator of Forests series in Fl. Mal. vol. 1).
