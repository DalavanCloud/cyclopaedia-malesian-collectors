Labohm, Johan Frederik

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1877, Amsterdam, Holland. Died: 1956, Bilthoven, Holland.

 

career:

Since 1903 Forest Officer in the employ of the D.E.I. Forest Service, stationed successively in the districts Soerabaja-Pasoeroean and S. Soerabaja; since 1913 in the teak districts of Central Java; in 1915 transferred to SE. Borneo (Bandjermasin), and since 1922 in Java again; he retired in 1932.

 

Collecting localities:

1915. Centr. Java: (D)Japara.-c. 1916-20. SE. Borneo: Sampanahar, Sepoea, Tandjong, Pleihari, Sampit, Balikpapan, Boentok, Martapoera, Asem Asem, P. Laoet, Samarinda, etc. 1922. W. Java: G. Salak above Masing (May).-1931. W. Java: Garoet.

 

collections:

Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: large collection of Borneo plants, numbered in the bb. series; Herb. Bog. [BO]: duplicates, also from Java.
