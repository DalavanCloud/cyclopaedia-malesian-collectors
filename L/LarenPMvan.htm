Laren, P.M. van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Assistant Resident of Tjiriengien, collected some plants in Kediri, E. Java, about 1860.

 

collections:

The material was originally sent to the ‘Natuurkundige Vereeniging’ at Batavia, from where it was forwarded to Binnendijk at Buitenzorg.1 If preserved, probably at Buitenzorg [BO] or Leiden [L].

 

literature:

(1) cf. Nat. Tijdschr. N.I. 21, 1860, p. 325.
