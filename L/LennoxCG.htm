Lennox, Colin Gordon

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Honolulu, was sponsored by the Hawaiian Sugar Planters’ Association for the collecting of seeds of wild relatives of the sugar cane in 1937.1 He was accompanied by C.E. Pemberton (see there for collecting localities etc.). No herbarium collections were made.

 

literature:

(1) C.G. Lennox: ‘Sugar cane collecting in New Guinea during 1937’ (Hawaiian Plant. Rec. 42, 1938, p. 235-246).
