Lesger, B. & W.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

One of them Manager of Timbang Langkat Estate in Sumatra East Coast.

 

Collecting localities:

1917-18. Sumatra East Coast: in the higher regions of G. Sibajak (1918), etc.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: from 1917 a 30 nos, and a 2nd collection in 1918.1 The plants were originally sent to the Garden at Sibolangit in Sumatra East Coast (branch of the Botanic Gardens, Buitenzorg), from where they were forwarded to Herb. Bog. [BO].

 

literature:
