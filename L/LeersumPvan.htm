Leersum, Pieter van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1854, Utrecht, Holland. Died: 1920, Amsterdam, Holland.

 

career:

Pharmacist, educated at Utrecht University; Chemist for the Cinchona culture in Java since 1884; in 1892 appointed Director of the Government Cinchona-plantation, Bandoeng, Java; retired in 1915.

 

collections:

Herb. Leiden [L]; Herb. Kol. Mus. (= Ind. Inst. Amsterdam): 23 nos Cinchona herbarium (pres. 1896); Herb. Groningen [now in L]: 26 species and varieties of Cinchona (pres. 1897).
