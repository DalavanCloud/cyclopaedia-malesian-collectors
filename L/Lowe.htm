Lowe

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is sometimes cited as the collector of plants in the Malay Peninsula and Borneo. Small collection from Borneo in Herb. Hooker (= Kew [K]) (cf. Hook. & Thoms., Flora Ind., 1, p. 71). Wrong for Low (see sub Hugh Low).
