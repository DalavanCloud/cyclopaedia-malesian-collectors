Lummel, Louise Elisabeth Aegidia van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1903, Batavia, Java.

 

career:

Zoologist, educated at Utrecht University (Ph. Dr 1930), who subsequently came to Java, working for some time (March 1931-Oct. ‘32)1 on P. Onrust2 in the Bay of Batavia; from 1932-33 Assistant at the Medical College, Batavia, and later teacher in Natural History at Medan (Sumatra). When returned to Holland, she took up the veterinary study.

 

Collecting localities:

W. Java, Bay of Batavia: islands of Purmerend and Kerkhof (Sept. 1932).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 5 nos of seagrasses.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Dammerman in Ann. Jard. Bot. Buit. 45, 1935, p. 52.

(2) cf. The Lloyd Mail Aug. 1932, p. 118.
