Uno, Abdul

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1900, Kwandang or Koeandang, N. Celebes.

 

career:

Employee of the D.E. Indian Forest Service since May 1924; respectively stationed in E. Java, in W. Java (Buitenzorg, Forest Research Institute), and in Central Java; since Sept. 1929 ‘boscharchitect’ (Head Overseer) in Gorontalo Subdivision (Manado Res., North Celebes). In 1947 he was transferred to E. Borneo.

Author of some typed reports on trips in Celebes.1

 

collections:

Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: N. and Centr. Celebes (Pos(s)o) plants (coll. 1930-38), numbered in the F.R.I. bb. series. Herb. Bog. [BO]: dupl. of the For. Res. Inst. and some herbaceous plants (nos 64 and 65, coll. Febr. and Aug. 1933).

 

literature:

(1) Viz on: Kwandang, Soemalati in Gorontalo (Aug. 22-24, 1931), Gorontalo (Dec. 1931, and 1933), Todjo region (Poso) (Apr. 5-28, 1935).
