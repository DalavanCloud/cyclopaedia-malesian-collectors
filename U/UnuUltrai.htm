Collections

 

Unu Ultrai

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is erroneously cited as the collector of Celtis latifolia (Blume) Planch. in Halmaheira, and of Trema orientalis var. rigida in Ternate (cf. Engl. Bot. Jahrb. 50, 1913, p. 311 and 322) of which the material is in Herb. Leiden [L]. The cited sheets are duplicates from Herb. Utrecht [U]. The labels read: ‘ex Herb. Univ. Ultraj.’, of which the last two words were misunderstood for a collector’s name.
