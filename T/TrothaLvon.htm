Collections

 

Trotha, Lothar von

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Herb. Berl. [B]: 20 siphonogams from Java.

In the same herbarium 320 Africa plants (coll. 1891-97) collected by Herr Oberst von Trotha, Commander of the ‘Schutztruppe’ in German East Africa (cf. Notizbl. Berl. Dahl. 1, 1897, p. 287). The collectors may be identical, but up to the present no data concerning his stay in Java came to my knowledge.
