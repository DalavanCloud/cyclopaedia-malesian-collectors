Togashi, Makoto

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Japanese professional plant collector. He collected for the ‘Medical Company Herbarium Laboratory Takeda’.

 

Collecting localities:

1961-62. Malay Peninsula, a.o. in Johore and Singapore.-1966. Borneo.

 

collections:1

In Kyoto Herb. [KYO], Fac. Science. He has an intricate way of numbering, e.g. 6222413 means 1962, 2nd month, 24th day, no 13.

 

literature:

(1) J. Ohwi: ‘Grasses of the Malay Peninsula and Singapore collected by Mr. M. Togasi’ (Bull. Nat. Sc. Mus. 6, 1962, p. 119-123).
