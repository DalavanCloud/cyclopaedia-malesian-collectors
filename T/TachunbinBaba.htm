Tachun bin Baba

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Sakai Forest Guard, in the Forest Department Malay Peninsula, 1929-47, tree climber and herbarium collector at the Forest Research Institute, Kepong. Temporarily he worked under the direction of the Forest Department Sarawak (cf. also sub Daud), at least in 1938.

 

collections:

Cf. sub Conservator of Forests series, Kepong [KEP].
