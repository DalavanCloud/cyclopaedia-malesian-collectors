Tangkilisan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Probably W. Tangkilisan, formerly in the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service (cf. sub Forest Research Institute), accompanied Kostermans on the expedition to Morotai (Moluccas) in 1949.

 

collections:

Material preserved in Herb. For. Res. Inst. Bogor [BZF] and in Herb. Bog. [BO]. His name occurs on labels.
