Takadu

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

A native of New Ireland (Bismarck Archipelago), who collected together with Sikin on behalf of F.R.R. Schlechter, when the latter botanized in New Guinea (1907-09).

Cadetia takadui Schltr. was named after him (cf. Backer, Verkl. Woordenb., 1936).

 

collections:

With Herb. Schlechter in Herb. Berl. [B].
