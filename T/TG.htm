T., G.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

The initials ‘G.T.’ (perhaps G.I.), together with the locality Singapore and the date December 1845, occur upon the label of a specimen in the Herbarium of the Roy. Bot. Gard. Kew [K], to which has been added ‘G. Thomson’. Who the collector was is unknown. Certainly it was not Dr Gideon Thomson of Madras (cf. Burkill in Gard. Bull. Str. Settlem. 4, 1927, p. 132).
