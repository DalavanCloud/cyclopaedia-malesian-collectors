Turner, J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

He made a small collection of plants in New Britain.1 The described Eucalyptus was collected near Spacious Bay, the material was sent to von Mueller at Melbourne [MEL] by the Sydney Bot. Garden.

 

literature:

(1) F. von Mueller: ‘Description of an hitherto unrecorded species of Eucalyptus from New Britain’ (in Australas. Journ. Pharm., July 1886).
