Tollens, E.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Of Blitar (E. Java). The collector may be identical with E. Tollens, who from 1903-04 was Acting Assistant Curator of the Buitenzorg Botanic Gardens.

 

collections:

Sent an inflorescence of Phalaenopsis fimbriata J.J.Sm. n.sp. to Herb. Bog. [BO] (cf. Bull. Jard. Bot. Buit. sér. 3, vol. 3, 1921, p. 300 seq.).
