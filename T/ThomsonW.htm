Thomson, William

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1901, Tjimahi, Java. Died: Jan. 15, 1944, Bandoeng, Java.

 

career:

Who, after having gained chemical practice at a sugar factory, in 1922 entered the employ of the Experiment Station for the Java Sugar Industry at Pasoeroean (E. Java); since 1940 in the rank of Chemist.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 1 no from G. Lamongan (E. Java, coll. 1930).
