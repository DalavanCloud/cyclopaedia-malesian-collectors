Oersipuny, Paulus Simon Petrus

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1900, Koetaradja, Sumatra.

 

career:

Since 1921 Overseer of Forests in D.E. Indian Government service; in May 1936 placed at the disposal of the Resident of the Moluccas, in charge of the supervision of the winning of cajaput oil in Boeroe, stationed at Namlea; in Aug. 1941 transferred to Bengkalis, E of Sumatra.

 

collections:

Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: some hundreds of bb. nos from Boeroe; Herb. Bog. [BO]: nos 11-83 from the vegetation of the cajaput fields (pres. 1937) and some herbaceous plants (pres. 1941), through the intermediary of the For. Res. Inst.
