Ogata, Dr Ken

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

Of the Forest Experiment Station, Meguro, Tokyo.

 

Collecting localities:

Collected a large number of wood samples with reference herbarium specimens in Sabah (2nd half 1968); the first half of the year he was in Malaya, collecting at least on G. Gajah and G. Tempurong in Perak (Feb. 14-15).
