Oosterzee, J.J. van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

From time to time sent living ornamental plants to Hort. Bog., in about 1883. Evidently he was a lover of plants; in the library of the Batavian Society for Arts and Sciences is a catalogue of living cultivated plants, which were offered for sale.1

 

collections:

In 1899 he sent dried material of ‘Kedji Beling’, one of the lithotriptic plants, to Herb. Leiden [L].

 

literature:

(1) ‘Plantenlijst der aan den heer J.J. van Oosterzee Jr to Pegangsaän, toebehoorende botanische colleceee’ (Batavia 1881).
