Biographical data

 

Olsen, Sven-Erik Sandermann

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1920, Helsingor, Denmark.

 

career:

Was educated at the Pharmaceutical Highschool, Copenhagen (cand. pharm. 1944). Pharmaceutical chemist, since 1966 at Vesterbro Apotek, Copenhagen.

 

Collecting localities:

He participated in the Danish ‘Noona Dan Expedition’ (see there for itinerary etc.) from July 31, 1961-Feb. 20, 1962.

 

biographical data:

Dansk Bot. Ark. 21, 1963, p. 45, 244 (bibliogr.).
