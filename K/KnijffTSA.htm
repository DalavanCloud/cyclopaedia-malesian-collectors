Knijff, Tjepke Siebern Ariën

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1891, Gouda, Z.H., Holland.

 

career:

School teacher, from 1919-37 serving in the D.E.I.; in the years 1923-25 successively stationed at Tondano and Makassar in Celebes, teaching at high schools (M.U.L.O.).

 

Collecting localities:

N. Celebes: Tondano and environs (1923-24); SW. Celebes: Makassar (1925).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 64 nos N. Celebes, etc.; in 1924 he presented seeds and living plants to Hort. Bog.

cf. also sub Kruyff.
