Koningen, H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1940, Amstelveen, Netherlands.

 

career:

When being in the army as a soldier, he made a small collection in New Guinea. Later gardener at Amstelveen.

 

Collecting localities:

7 plants at Remu near Sorong (Jan. 1961) and near Ifar (Cyclops Mts, July 1961), W. New Guinea.

 

collections:

In Herb. Leiden [L].
