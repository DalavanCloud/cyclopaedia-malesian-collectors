Kievit, H.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Of Ambon, captain of the government steamer ‘Tomalhaut’, evidently brought home orchids from E. Borneo (Koetei), from P. Watkoesa (subdiv. of Ceram) and from Merauke (S. New Guinea).

 

collections:

Hort. Bog.: orchids from abovementioned localities (pres. 1924 and 1926, partly by Mrs Kievit).
