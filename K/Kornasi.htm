Kornas(s)i

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

For several years ‘mantri’ in the Botanic Gardens, Buitenzorg.

 

Collecting localities:

1910. Noesa Kambangan (S of Centr. Java) (Nov. 17-29) with SArëan.-1911. Banka (Aug.-Dec.)-1912. Noesa Kambangan (S of Java): G. Klètjèr.1-1913. Leaving Buitenzorg (July 2) for Dutch N. New Guinea as assistant of Dr Janowski (itiner. etc. see there). In the course of the year 1914 invalided home.-Rutten Expedition, 1917-18.2 Moluccas, Ceram: Wahai (arriving Aug. 18, 1917), for localities etc. cf. sub Rutten; visit to Ambon (Apr. 1-25, 1918); back at Buitenzorg (Aug. 21).-W Java: environs Buitenzorg (1920); Soebang (1926).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO], cf. also sub Janowski and Rutten. Herb. Leiden [L]: dupl. of New Guinea and the Moluccas; Herb. Utrecht [U]: dupl. Ceram (1918).

In Hort. Bog.: 240 living plants from Ceram; others from Noesa Kambangan, Banka, etc.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Bull. Jard. Bot. Buit. sér. 3, vol. 1, 1918, p. 370 and Versl. Pl. Tuin Buitenzorg for 1919, p. 224.

(2) cf. Versl. l.c. for 1918, p. 228.
