Kokawa, Shohei

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1927.

 

career:

Of Osaka City University.

 

Collecting localities:

Spent 5 months (Oct. 1968-Mar. 1969) in many areas of the state Sabah (N. Borneo), with Ando and Hotta (see there). Special attention was given to Tawau Hills and Silabukan in the south and to Kinabalu and Trus Madi on the west coast.
