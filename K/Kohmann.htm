Kohmann, Dr

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is cited as the collector of Wendlandia glabrata DC. near Batavia in 1838.1 The specimen concerned is no 566 of his collection and is preserved in the Herb. Brit. Mus. [BM].

The name Kohmann is probably wrong for Kollmann (see sub G.J.A. Kollmann), part of whose plants came to the Brit. Museum with Herb. Shuttleworth.

 

literature:

(1) cf. Cowan in Not. Roy. But. Gard. Edinb. 16, 1932, p. 276 and in Bull. Jard. Bot. Buit. sér. 3, vol. 14, 1936, p. 20.
