Kievits, Dirk Baldus Jan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1889, Nijkerk, Gld, Holland.

 

career:

Agriculturist, educated at Wageningen Agricultural College; since 1912 in the employ of the Experiment Station for the Java Sugar Industry (Pasoeroean); stationed respectively at Djokja (1912-15), Banjoemas (1915-25), Djombang (1925-28), and at Banjoemas again (1928-32). Subsequently he retired and settled at Zeist, Holland.

 

Collecting localities:

Centr. Java: Banjoemas (1923-25), Kedoe (1924) and Banjoemas (1931).

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 1396 nos (ranging from below 100 to upwards of 3500); some dupl. in Herb. Leiden [L], and in Herb. Univ. Amsterdam [AMD].
