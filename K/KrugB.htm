Collections

 

Krug, B.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Collected plants on P. Weh, N of Sumatra, e.g. at Sabang; evidently preserved in Herb. Berl. [B], at least the nos 7 (Ixora sabangensis Bremek.), 63 (Cyperus kyllingia Endl.) and 66 (Selaginella mayeri Alst.).

Plants from a certain Bertram Krug, Government Gardener at Tsingtau, from China (a. 1905-13) in Herb. Berl. [B]. He may be the collector of the above-mentioned plants too.
