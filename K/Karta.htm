(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Indonesian, temporarily in the employ of the Botanic Gardens at Buitenzorg. He attended Kjellberg’s expedition to Celebes, 1929-30, and after that was reluctant to do outdoor work.

 

Collecting localities:

1928. With Dammerman Expedition (see there) to Bawean (May 4-13). With Beumée (see there) to Sibolangit, Sumatra East Coast (Dec.). 1930. With M.A. Lieftinck (see there) to the Karimoendjawa Islands: P. Karimoendjawa (Nov. 23-27; Nov. 23 also on P. Mendiangan besar); P. Glean (= P. Keleang; 27); P. Kemoedjan (29-30).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 166 Bawean nos,1 81 Sumatra nos, and from Karimoendjawa the nos 167-420; Herb. Leiden [L]: Karimoendjawa dupl. Living plants in Hort. Bog.

 

literature:

(1) cf. O. Posthumus: ‘Ferns of Bawean’ (Proc. Roy. Ac. Sci. Amsterdam 32, 1929, 9 pp.).
