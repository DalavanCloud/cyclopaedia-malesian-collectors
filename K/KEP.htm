KEP nos

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

information:

A series of the Forest Research Institute at Kepong, Malaya. As it reached the six figures by 1965, a new series with the prefix FRI was begun in 1966; the first number is FRI 0001. The junior staff continued (at least still in 1967) with the KEP series. [See also Conservator of Forests (C.F.) series]
