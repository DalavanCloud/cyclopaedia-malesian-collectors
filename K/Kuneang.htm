Kuneang

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

career:

Probably spelled this way, was an untrained Dyak employed as collector by G.D. Haviland in Sarawak, NW. Borneo, for a couple of months. Labels with his name are not very reliable (vide letter from Haviland to the Director at Kew, dated Sept. 30, 1891).
