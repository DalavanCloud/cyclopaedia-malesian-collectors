Collections

 

Kadim bin Tassim

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Collecting localities:

collected with J. Sinclair in N. Borneo (June-July 1957); with J. Carrick & I. Enoch in Sarawak: Bako National Park (Apr. 18-May 11, 1959). In Malaya with Mahmud bin Awang in Pahang (Mar.-Apr. 1959), with Mohd Nur in Johore (June-July 1959), with Mrs Allen (Aug. 1959), with R.K. Dentan in Perak (Feb. and Apr. 1962). He must have collected in several other instances.

 

collections:

Herb. Sing. [SING]; dupl. elsewhere, a.o. Herb. Edinb. [E].
