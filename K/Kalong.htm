Collections

 

Kalong

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Collected Pilea rigidiuscula C.B.Rob., no 1721, in Sarawak (cf. Merrill, Enum. Born. Pl. 1921, p. 230). Evidently a native collector (E.G. = Forest Guard) of the Malay Forest Department, of whom dupl. are in Herb. Edinburgh [E].
