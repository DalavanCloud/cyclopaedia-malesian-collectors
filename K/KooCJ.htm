Koo, Chang Jee

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Died: 1932, Kuching, Sarawak.

 

career:

A Chinaman, clerk and taxidermist of the Sarawak Museum at Kuching, NW. Borneo. According to Mjöberg1 in the employ of the Museum since its foundation in 1891. He retired in 1927.

 

collections:

Herb. Sarawak [SAR]: from Mt Poi (July 1908), etc.

 

literature:

(1) cf. E. Mjöberg: ‘Borneo, bet land der koppensnellers’ p. 157-158, w. portr.
