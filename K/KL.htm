InformatioN:

A series of the Phytochemical Survey of the Federation of Malaya, was started in 1957 by Millard. It has been resumed by Dr J. Carrick, Field Pharmacologist, with number 2332. Some confusion has arisen because of the three numbers attached to the old series (KL, PCSM, and a herb. number). The first number, KL, is the only one that is continuous.
