Kerbosch, Matthieu Gerard Jaques Marie

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1880, Venlo, L., Holland. Died: ± 1973, Soest, U., Holland.

 

career:

Pharmacist who took his Dr’s degree in 1910, a a subsequently was appointed Government Chemist of the Agricultural Department at Buitenzorg; in 1914 Assistant Director of the Government Cinchona plantation, Tjinjiroean, and since 1915 Director of that plantation and besides of the Cinchona Experiment Station at Pengalengan; retired in 1925.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: dried material of cultivated Cinchona species (pres. 1916).
