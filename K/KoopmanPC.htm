Koopman, Pietertje Cornelia

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1901, Nieuwerkerk, Z., Holland.

 

career:

Studied biology at Utrecht University (1919-24); from 1924-26 teacher in natural history at a secondary school at Tilburg (Br.). In 1926 she married and went to Java. From 1938 onwards teacher at Buitenzorg. After the war she returned to Holland and was appointed at a secondary school in Amsterdam.

 

Collecting localities:

When staying in the Mountain Gardens, Tjibodas, W. Java (June 29-July 18, and Dec. 1941), she collected c. 125 nos.

 

collections:

In Herb. Bog. [BO].
