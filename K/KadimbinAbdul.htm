Kadim bin Abdul (not Kadir bin Abdul)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1918, Labuk, N. Borneo.

 

career:

Was appointed as Labourer in the For. Dept Br. N. Borneo in 1937; since 1951 Forest Guard.

 

collections:

Mostly botanical collections in various parts of Br. N. Borneo [SAN]. Cf. sub Forest Department British North Borneo.
