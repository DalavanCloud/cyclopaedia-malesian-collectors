Kuntze, Carl Ernest Otto

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1843, Leipsic, Germany. Died: 1907, San Remo, Italy.

 

career:

Merchant-botanist who made a voyage round the world (see below), during which the Malayan region was visited. Later he travelled in Turkestan, S. America (1891-92), and in Africa (1894).1 In 1905 he settled at San Remo.

Several plants were named after him.

 

Collecting localities:

Voyage round the world, 1874-76.2 Visiting West India, America, China, Annam, Cochin-China; in 1875 in the Malaysian region: Singapore; Java (May 2-Sept. 27), viz in W. Java: Batavia; Buitenzorg (May 12-13), visiting Kuripan etc.; departing for Sindanglaja (24), Tjibodas, G. Gedeh-Pangrango (30) and Telaga Warna; Tjiandjur (June 5); Wijnkoopsbaai, Palabuan Ratu, etc. (10-16); Sukabumi (17-21); S. Priangan (_ prob. S of Sukabumi) (June 22-July 1); Sukabumi (2); via Tjiandjur to Bandung, visiting G. Tangkuban Prahu (10), Pengalengan and Tjinjiruan (12); Cheribon (17-19); Centr. Java: Pekalongan, Semarang; Demak (28), Purwodadi, Kuwu, Demak, Semarang (31); Magelang (Aug. 6), Borobudur, G. Sumbing (13), Wonosobo, Diëng Plateau (Aug. 16-19, ascending G. India (Himalaya), Aden and Egypt; back at Leipsic (Febr. 1876).

 

collections:

His collection 1874-76 amounts to c. 7700 plants in 30.000 specimens,3 at present in Herb. N. Y. Bot. Gard. [NY].4 Duplicates at Munich [M]; Herb. Kew [K]: 1140 nos (pres. 1887-94); Herb. Berl. [B]: 330 nos voyage 1874-76 and 35 nos in the Show Mus.; Herb. Vienna [W] (with Herb. Reichenbach): orchids from the East Indies; Herb. Leiden [L]; U.S. Nat. Herb. Wash. [US]: 13 Java nos; Herb. Edinb. [E]: 1 bundle of plants.
