Kock, Albert Hendrik Wendelin Baron de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1808, Soerabaja, Java. Died: 1891.

 

career:

In 1841 Lieutenant Colonel, acting Resident of Palembang, S. Sumatra; since 1848 stationed in Java, finally Vice President of the Council of the D.E.I.; retired in 1860.

 

collections:

He sent some fine Calamus specimens to Hort. Bog. (see letter from Teijsmann to de Vriese, dated Nov. 27, 1847, in the archives of Leiden Herbarium).
