Weber, Gustav

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

District Judge in former Kaiser-Wilhelmsland, NE. New Guinea, where he collected on the mainland (1912), and in Neu Pommern (= New Britain) in the Bismarck Archipelago (1912-13).

 

collections:

Herb. Berl. [B]: 27 nos from NE. New Guinea (Sattelberg and Finschhafen), 10 nos from New Britain (Rabaul).
