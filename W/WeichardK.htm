Weichard, Dr Karl

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

collections:

Is cited by Schuster (in Pflanzenreich Heft 99, 1932) as the collector of Cycas circinalis ssp. papuana in former German (= NE. New Guinea), in Nov. 1890. Probably the label was misread, the actual collector being Weinland (see there).
