Weismann, O.Th.J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Entered the employ of the D.E. Indian Forest Service on monthly pay; in 1935 appointed Forest Officer. He was stationed in Celebes and Dependencies, and operated in Boeton and Laiwoei (SE. Celebes), later in the employ of these self-governing districts.

 

Collecting localities:

Centr. Celebes: Malili (Dec. 21, 1932);1 P. Moena (1936); SE. Celebes (1938 and 1941).

 

collections:

Herb. For. Res. Inst. Buitenzorg [BZF]: F.R.I. bb. nos (see sub Forest Research Inst.); also in Herb. Bog. [BO].

 

literature:

(1) cf. Bloembergen in Blumea 1, 1935, p. 287.
