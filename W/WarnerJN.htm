Warner, John Northrup

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Born: 1919, Los Angeles, Cal., U.S.A.

 

career:

Was educated in the Universities of Hawaii (B.S. 1941) and Minnesota (Ph.D. 1950, Plant Genetics and Statistics). From 1941 to present employed by the Experiment Station of the Hawaiian Sugar Planters’ Association, at present Senior Geneticist.

 

Collecting localities:

For his visits to New Guinea see respectively sub J.H. Buzacott and C.O. Grassl.
