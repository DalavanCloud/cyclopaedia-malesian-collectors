Wethmar, Alexander Godert

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1893, Makassar, Celebes.

 

career:

Was educated at the Agricultural College, Wageningen (Holland); from 1919-22 Consulting Agriculturist at Buitenzorg (W. Java) and Tapanoeli (NW. Sumatra); subsequently on the staff of the Java Sugar Experiment Station at Pasoeroean, as Group-Adviser at Kediri from 1923-37, and ditto at Djokja from 1937-41; since 1941 Agriculturist at Pasoeroean; retired in 1947.

 

collections:

Herb. Pasoer. [PAS now in BO]: 12 nos from Kediri, E. Java (coll. 1927-28).
