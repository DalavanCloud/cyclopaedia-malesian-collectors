Weeda, Jan

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1910, Neurenberg, Bavaria, Germany.

 

career:

Was educated in Holland; geologist, taking his Ph.Dr at Leiden in 1936; since 1936 in the employ of the B.P.M. (Batavian Oil Co.). At present of the Sarawak Oil Fields Ltd, Serian, Brunei.

 

collections:

Herb. Leiden [L]: some 13 plants in Br. N. Borneo, on P. Mengaloen (Mangalum) (coll. July-Aug. 1947).
