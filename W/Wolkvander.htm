Wolk, van der

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Possibly the collector is Pieter Cornelis van der Wolk (born: 1883, Tebingtinggi, Sumatra) who was appointed Assistant in the Division for Plant Diseases at Buitenzorg in 1912. Since 1922 he was teacher in natural history at Batavia, and from 1927-35 ditto at Soerabaja.

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 11 plants (coll. March 1914), from ? Java.
