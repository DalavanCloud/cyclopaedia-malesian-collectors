Walandouw, E.K.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Physician on an estate of the H.V.A. (Handelsvereeniging Amsterdam) on the slope of G. Kerintji at Kajoe Aro, Soengei Penoeh (Sumatra West Coast).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: 29 nos of plants from a pond at Soengei Assem (2 km from Danau Bento), Sumatra West Coast (collected early in 1934). The material was presented through the intermediary of Keuchenius (see there).
