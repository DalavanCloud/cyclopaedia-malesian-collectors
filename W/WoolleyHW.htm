Woolley, Hubert Worsley

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1887, London, England.

 

career:

Forest Officer, educated at Oxford (B.A.) and at Emmanuel College, Cambridge; in the Forest Dept Malay Peninsula, 1921-34.

 

collections:

He did little collecting, mainly in Pahang and Negri Sembilan, Malay Peninsula; in Herb. Kuala Lump. [KEP], cf. sub Conservator of Forest series, Kepong.
