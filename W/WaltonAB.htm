Walton, Arthur Bernard

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1904, U.K.

 

career:

Was educated in forestry at Edinburgh University (B.S.); from 1926-1955 a member of the Malayan Forest Service, collecting occasionally in the C.F. series (cf. Conservator of Forests series in Fl. Mal. vol. 1).

 

biographical data:
