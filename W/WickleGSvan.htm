Wickle, G.S. van

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Forest Officer of the Forestry Bureau, Manila; P.I., is cited as the collector of the nos 897 and 701 in Luzon (P.I.) by Merrill in Enum. Philip. Fl. Pl.

 

collections:

In 1903/04, 21 nos of plants collected by him in Prov. of Camarines Sur, Luzon, were presented to Herb. Manila [PNH].
