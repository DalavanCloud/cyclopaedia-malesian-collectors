Wigman Jr, Herman Jan (not Hendrik Jan)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1879, Buitenzorg, Java. Died: 1957, Voorschoten, Holland.

 

career:

Son of the former, from 1902-30 Assistant Curator of the Buitenzorg Botanic Gardens; subsequently retired and living in Holland.

 

collections:

From tours in W. Java he brought living plants on behalf of Hort. Bog., viz from: Tjibodas & Pangrango (1907), Tjibodas and Koeripan (1910), Kawah Manoek (1911), Tjibodas (ferns, 1912), etc. His name may appear on some herbarium labels, though this is uncertain.
