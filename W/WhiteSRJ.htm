White, S.R.J.

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Collecting localities:

Made a collection of seeds, when travelling through Indonesia to Singapore, for W.H. Lewis.1 They were collected in Java: Bandung, Timor: Atambua, Singapore.

 

collections:

Seeds of Oldenlandia corymbosa L. (= Hedyotis corymbosa (L.) Lam.). Not known whether he made other collections. The plants resulting from the seeds were given Lewis numbers.1

 

literature:

(1) W.H. Lewis: ‘Chromosome numbers of Oldenlandia corymbosa (Rub.) from Southeastern Asia’ (Ann. Mo. Bot. Gard. 53, 1966, p. 257-258).
