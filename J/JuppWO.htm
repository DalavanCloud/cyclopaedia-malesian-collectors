Jupp, W.O. (or W.D.)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

career:

Of the China Borneo Company, resided for a period of some twenty years in Br. N. Borneo. Diospyros juppi Merr. was named after him.

 

collections:

 The above-mentioned plant was collected by him in Sept. 1919, and was numbered 727.1 Possibly numbered in the series of the Forest Department British North Borneo (see there, also for distribution of sets [SAN]).

 

literature:

(1) cf. Merrill in Journ. As. Soc. Mal. Br. 1, 1923, p. 26.
