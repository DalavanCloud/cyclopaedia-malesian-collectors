Joncheere, Gerardus Johannus de

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1909, Den Heider, N.H., Holland.

 

career:

Since 1931 in the employ of the K.P.M. (Royal Packet Company) in the D.E.I.; since 1947 staying in Europe. He came to collecting ferns after the publication of the book on the ferns of Java by Backer & Posthumus.

 

Collecting localities:

W. Java: Batavia (1940-41); E. Java: Prigen (1941), collecting Egenolfla bipinnatifida near waterfall on the NE. slope of G. Ardjoeno; W. Java: Tjimahi, in prisoner’s camp (1942).

 

collections:

Private Herbarium, lost now.
