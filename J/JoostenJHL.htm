Joosten, Johannes Hubertus Leonardus

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

 

Born: 1904, Venlo, L., Holland.

 

career:

Studied horticulture at the Agricultural College, Wageningen; since 1928 appointed Consulting Agriculturist in the D.E.I., stationed in Java, respectively at Buitenzorg and Pasoeroean (1928), Malang (1929-30), Blitar (1930-32), Madioen (1932-35), and Batavia (1935- . . ); in 1947 temporary Inspector of Agricultural Instruction.

 

Collecting localities:

1928. E. Java: Kraksaän and Pasoeroean (Sept. 19), Bangil (Oct.).

 

collections:

Herb. Bog. [BO]: some Loranthaceae.
