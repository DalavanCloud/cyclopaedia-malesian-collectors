Jespersen, Julius

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 1: Cyclopaedia of collectors)

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 5: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement I)

 

Born: 1859, near Lemvig, Denmark. Died: 1950.

 

career:

Passed his final medical examination in 1885; army surgeon in the Dutch East Indies from 1886-90, civil at Pasoeroean from 1892-1902; since Nov. 1902 settled at Skovshuved, Denmark.

 

collections:

Herb. Copenhagen [C]: plants from Banka and Java (pres. 1889, 1891, 1899).

 

biographical data:

‘Den Danske Lagestand 1949-1957’, 13th ed. 1957, p. 851 with portr.
