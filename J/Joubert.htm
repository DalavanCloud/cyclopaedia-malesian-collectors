Joubert

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

Collecting localities:

Collected 18 varieties of sugar-cane on Tabar I. and New Ireland in 1921. He was sponsored by the Colonial Sugar Refining Co of Australia.1

 

collections:

Whether any herbarium specimens were made is unknown to me.
