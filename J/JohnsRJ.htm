Johns, Robert J. (Bob)

 

(Source: Flora Malesiana ser. 1, 8: Cyclopaedia of collectors, Supplement II)

 

career:

M.Sc.; employed in the Division of Botany at Lae, Terr. of New Guinea, at present teacher at Bulolo Forestry College.

Joint author of a Check list of Mount Wilhelm Flora.1

 

Collecting localities:

He accompanied Dr B.O. van Zanten (see there) on a trip in E. New Guinea in Oct.-Nov. 1969.

 

collections:

He probably collected in both the NGF and LAE series [LAE].

 

literature:

(1) R.J. Johns & P.F. Stevens: ‘Mount Wilhelm Flora: A Check List of Species’ (Bot. Bull. no 6, 1971, 60 + 4 pp., 7 fig., 1 map).
